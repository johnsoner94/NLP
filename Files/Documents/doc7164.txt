      <li>

      <li>

       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://histories.cambridge.org/uid=1931/collection?id=cat_religious_studies">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://histories.cambridge.org/uid=1931/collection?id=cat_religious_studies
       </a>
       <br />
       Treats the major historical, political and cultural developments in Jewish history and the history of Judaism.
      </li>
      <li>
       Cambridge History of the Bible
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://histories.cambridge.org/uid=1931/collection?id=cat_religious_studies">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://histories.cambridge.org/uid=1931/collection?id=cat_religious_studies
       </a>
       <br />

      </li>
      <li>
       Credo Reference
       <br />
       <a href="http://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.credoreference.com">
        http://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.credoreference.com
       </a>
       <br />
       Hundreds of authoritative dictionaries, encyclopedias, fact books, guides, companions and more from respected publishers across dozens of major subject areas.
      </li>
      <li>
       Encyclopaedia of Judaism
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/oclc/764567613?databaseList=1708,638">
        https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/oclc/764567613?databaseList=1708,638
       </a>
       <br />
       Purports to provide a full and reliable account of Judaism, beginning in ancient Israelite times and extending to our own day.
      </li>
      <li>
       Encyclopedia of Buddhism
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://find.galegroup.com/openurl/openurl?url_ver=Z39.88-2004&amp;res_id=info%3Asid%2Fgale%3AGVRL&amp;url_ctx_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:ctx&amp;ctx_enc=info:ofi:enc:UTF-8&amp;rft_val_fmt=info:ofi%2Ffmt:kev:mtx:journal&amp;rft.isbn=9780028659107&amp;req_dat=info:sid/gale:oclc.org:mul_coll">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://find.galegroup.com/openurl/openurl?url_ver=Z39.88-2004&res;_id=info%3Asid%2Fgale%3AGVRL&url;_ctx_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:ctx&ctx;_enc=info:ofi:enc:UTF-8&rft;_val_fmt=info:ofi%2Ffmt:kev:mtx:journal&amp;rft.isbn;=9780028659107&req;_dat=info:sid/gale:oclc.org:mul_coll
       </a>
       <br />
       This encyclopedia describes the Buddhist world view, basic teachings and practices of Buddhism, as well as its different schools and sects. In addition to containing entries on Buddhist scriptures, art, architecture, divinities, monastic orders, festivals, rites and ceremonies, this 2-vol. set explores the history of Buddhism, the different forms it has taken in different parts of the world, and how Buddhism has blended with other religions.
      </li>
      <li>
       Encyclopedia of Religion
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://find.galegroup.com/openurl/openurl?url_ver=Z39.88-2004&amp;url_ctx_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:ctx&amp;req_dat=info:sid/gale:ugnid:mul_coll&amp;res_id=info:sid/gale:GVRL&amp;ctx_enc=info:ofi:enc:UTF-8&amp;rft_val_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:book&amp;rft_id=info:sid/gale:bmcode:recid/5BSX">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://find.galegroup.com/openurl/openurl?url_ver=Z39.88-2004&url;_ctx_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:ctx&req;_dat=info:sid/gale:ugnid:mul_coll&res;_id=info:sid/gale:GVRL&ctx;_enc=info:ofi:enc:UTF-8&rft;_val_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:book&rft;_id=info:sid/gale:bmcode:recid/5BSX
       </a>
       <br />
       The second edition of a resource that is considered a standard reference in the field. Presents a cross-cultural approach that emphasizes religion's role within everyday life and as a unique experience from culture to culture. The original 2,750 entries have been retained, many heavily updated, and approximately 600 entirely new articles have been added by an international team of scholars and contributors.
      </li>
      <li>
       New Encyclopedia of Judaism
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/oclc/436848559?databaseList=1708,638">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/oclc/436848559?databaseList=1708,638
       </a>
       <br />
       A comprehensive one-volume encyclopedia that accessibly presents every aspect of the Jewish religion and represents current thinking among scholars in the Reform, Conservative, and Orthodox movements.
