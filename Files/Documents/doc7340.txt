          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="roundbox innerbox innerbox_1" id="content21681411">
          <p>
           <strong>
            SCHOLARLY, PEER-REVIEWED ARTICLES
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <a href="http://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=aph">
            Academic Search Complete
           </a>
           <br />

           <strong>
            Check for Full Text
           </strong>
           <span>
            &nbsp;buttons
           </span>
           &nbsp;to check for full text online or in the library. Use the Academic Journals filter to narrow your search results to scholarly sources.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <a href="http://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/">
            Trexler Library Subject Guides
            <br />
           </a>
           Subject Guides point you to recommended resources in each subject area. Look for the subject area related to your research interest and then for databases recommended for that subject.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            POPULAR ARTICLES
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <a href="http://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=aph">
            Academic Search Complete
           </a>
           <br />
           A database of both scholarly and popular publications in a wide variety of disciplines and subject areas. Most full text is linked directly in the database; otherwise use the
           <strong>
            Check for Full Text
           </strong>
           <span>
            &nbsp;buttons
           </span>
           &nbsp;to check for full text online or in the library. Use the Magazines and/or Newspapers filters to narrow your search results to popular sources.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <a href="http://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.lexisnexis.com/hottopics/lnacademic">
            LexisNexis Academic
           </a>
           <br />
           A database of popular newspapers, magazines, and broadcast transcripts, as well as business publications, legal materials, and more. Full text is linked directly in the database.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            GOVERNMENT SOURCES
           </strong>
           <br />
           <br />
           NOTE: Take note of government agencies, government documents, and other government-related references in your scholarly and popular sources that may be helpful in directing your searching here.
          </p>
          <p>
           NOTE: Access to government documents from other countries via these resources depends largely on how available each country makes them.&nbsp; Language may prove to be another barrier.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <a href="https://www.google.com/">
            Google
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <a href="http://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.lexisnexis.com/hottopics/lnacademic">
            LexisNexis Academic
           </a>
           <a href="http://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=aph">
            <br />
           </a>
           A database of popular newspapers, magazines, and broadcast transcripts, as well as business publications, legal materials, and more. Full text is linked directly in the database. Look for BBC Monitoring reports under News Transcripts, especially, to get translations of government press releases and other sources originating from governments.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <a href="http://www.law.cornell.edu/world/">
            Legal Information Institute, Law by Source: Global
           </a>
           (Cornell University)
           <br />
           The LII collection of world legal materials gathers, country by country, continent by continent, the Internet-accessible sources of the constitutions, statutes, judicial opinions, and related legal material from around the globe (excluding the U.S. material held in the LII's other collections). It also holds resources and document collections of International Law.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <a href="http://www.washlaw.edu/forint/index.html">
            WashLaw Web: Foreign and International Law
           </a>
           (Washburn University School of Law Library)
           <br />
           Links to foreign and international legal resources and research tools. It's especially strong on finding legal materials from specific countries
          </p>
