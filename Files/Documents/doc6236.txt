           <h3>
            Pre-K-4 Certification
           </h3>
           <p>
            The Pre-K-4 certification permits the candidate to teach in early childhood settings as well elementary schools up to grade 4. There are a total of 14 education courses along with the cognate and major courses. &nbsp;
            <br />
            <br />
           </p>
           <div class="content-add05 style4">
            <ul>
             <li class="content-add05">
              A bachelor&rsquo;s degree in any&nbsp;
              <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/academics/majorsandprograms.html">
               major
              </a>
              offered at Muhlenberg
             </li>
             <li class="content-add05">
              Required Cognate Courses
             </li>
             <li style="list-style: none;">
              <ul>
               <li class="content-add05">
                2 courses in mathematics (one must be Fundamentals of Mathematics or Shape &amp; Symmetry)
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Writing Composition
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                British or American Literature
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Biology
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Physical Science
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Foundations of the Creative Arts
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                World Geography
               </li>
              </ul>
             </li>
             <li class="content-add05">
              Education Courses:
             </li>
             <li style="list-style: none;">
              <ul>
               <li class="content-add05">
                History and Politics of American Education
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Educational Psychology: Child Learning and Development
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Introduction to Special Education
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Introduction to Early Childhood Education
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Integrating Curriculum and Instruction for Young Learners
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Theory and Practice of Teaching English Language Learners
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Language and Early Literacy Education
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Literacy &amp; Social Studies Education
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Mathematics Education for Young Learners
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Science Education for Young Learners
               </li>
              </ul>
             </li>
             <li class="content-add05">
              Professional Semester:
             </li>
             <li style="list-style: none;">
              <ul>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Assessment and Evaluation
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Studies in Professional Education
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Student Teaching I
               </li>
               <li class="content-add05">
                Student Teaching II
               </li>
              </ul>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             If you are interested, please contact our office to schedule an initial meeting with the Director of Teacher Certification.&nbsp;A copy of your resume and any transcripts you may have will be helpful in assessing what courses you will need to take. Additionally, you might want to download these files:
             <br />
             <br />
             <a href="/media/contentassets/pdf/wescoe/programcourseofferings/teachercert/WEB - Teachers Certification - FAQ - FINAL 020416.pdf">
              Teacher Certification Frequently Asked Questions
             </a>
             <br />
             <a href="/media/contentassets/pdf/wescoe/programcourseofferings/teachercert/WEB - Teachers Certification - 11x17 - FINAL 020416.pdf">
              Teacher Certification Brochure
             </a>
             <br />
             <br />
             <a href="/main/wescoe/prospectivestudents/appointment.html">
              I would like to schedule an advisement meeting
             </a>
             .
             <br />
             <br />
             If you have any additional questions or would like to speak with an academic advisor, please contact us at 484-664-3300 or&nbsp;
             <a href="mailto:wescoe@muhlenberg.edu">
              wescoe@muhlenberg.edu
             </a>
             .
            </p>

For others, an associate's degree provides the focused, two-year experience necessary to succeed. No matter what your plans, our academic advisors are always there to help you chart your course of study.
