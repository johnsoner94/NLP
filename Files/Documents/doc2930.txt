          <div style="line-height: 160%; font-size:120%;">
           <p style="font-size:115%; color:#7a0109; margin-bottom:8px;">
            <b>
             <i>
              <a href="documents/Recruit-brochure-2015-web-s.pdf" style="color:#7a0109;">
               Download the Department Brochure
              </a>
             </i>
            </b>
            [PDF, 1.9 mb]
           </p>
           <p style="font-size:150%; color:#7a0109; margin-bottom:8px;">
            <b>
             A Complete Education
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            At Muhlenberg Theatre &amp; Dance, you'll receive
            <b>
             intensive studio training
            </b>
            and incredible
            <b>
             performance and design
             <a href="production.html">
              opportunities
             </a>
            </b>
            . But you will also get a first-rate liberal arts education, from one of the
            <b>
             best small liberal arts colleges
            </b>
            in the country. At Muhlenberg, we think breadth of knowledge and experience and excellent studio training are equally important.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            <b>
             If you are planning a
             <a href="../curriculum/dual.html">
              dual major
             </a>
             ,
            </b>
            or you want to explore other careers in addition to the performing arts, then the choice becomes even clearer. Here you'll have the
            <b>
             freedom of academic exploration
            </b>
            &mdash; and the opportunity to pursue
            <i>
             all
            </i>
            your goals.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            <b>
             <a href="../curriculum/index.html">
              Explore the Theatre &amp; Dance Curriculum...
             </a>
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size:150%; color:#7a0109; margin-bottom:8px; margin-top:18px;">
            <b>
             World-Class Teachers
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            <b>
             Your professors and instructors
            </b>
            are accomplished, respected artists in their fields &mdash; designers, directors, choreographers, dancers, and actors who pursue active, vibrant careers.
            <b>
             <a href="../aboutus/facstaff.html">
              See for yourself!
             </a>
            </b>
            In addition, each year we bring in
            <b>
             <a href="../aboutus/guestartists.html">
              acclaimed guest artists
             </a>
            </b>
            to teach, direct, choreograph, design, and perform.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            <b>
             These artists will be your teachers, your mentors, your collaborators, and your colleagues,
            </b>
            working one-on-one with you in the classroom, in the studio, and on the stage. And when you contact us to
            <b>
             <a href="request.html">
              schedule a visit,
             </a>
            </b>
            the people you talk to will be the directors and founders of the Department. How's that for access?
           </p>
           <p style="font-size:150%; color:#7a0109; margin-bottom:8px; margin-top:18px;">
            <b>
             First-Rate Facilities
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            <b>
             You'll use state-of-the-art
             <a href="../facilities/index.html">
              performance facilities
             </a>
            </b>
            , including two large theatres and two blackbox-style studio theatres, all equipped with modern lighting and sound, as well as expansive dance and acting studio space for classes, rehearsals, and student projects.
           </p>
           <p style="font-size:150%; color:#7a0109; margin-bottom:8px; margin-top:18px;">
            <b>
             Amazing Opportunities to Perform &amp; Create
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            <b>
             The Department presents a
             <a href="../onstage/mainstage.html">
              Mainstage Season
             </a>
            </b>
            of six theatre productions and three dance concerts, from October through May, ranging in style from experimental studio productions to major Broadway musicals.
            <b>
             More than 100 students are cast
            </b>
            each season in Mainstage shows and dance concerts, which also offer numerous opportunities for
            <b>
             directing, choreography, stage management, and design
            </b>
            students.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            In addition, the student-run
            <a href="http://muhlenbergmta.wix.com/bergmta" target="_blank">
             Muhlenberg Theatre Association
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://sites.google.com/site/muhlenbergdance/" target="_blank">
             Muhlenberg Dance Association
            </a>
            produce an annual series of
            <b>
             informal dance concerts and studio productions
            </b>
            that give students opportunities to direct, choreograph, design, and perform, and several
            <b>
             touring theatre and dance ensembles
            </b>
            take the show on the road to area schools community centers.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            <b>
             <a href="../smt/index.html">
              Summer Music Theatre
             </a>
            </b>
            employs between 50 and 100 students each summer, as actors, stage managers, carpenters, stitchers, and administrative staff, giving participants hands-on
            <b>
             professional summer stock experience.
            </b>
            Many students also participate in the College Choir, a cappella groups, dance ensembles, and instrumental groups across campus.
           </p>
           <p style="font-size:150%; color:#7a0109; margin-bottom:8px; margin-top:18px;">
            <b>
             International Study
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            <b>
             The Department offers incredible opportunities
            </b>
            for Theatre &amp; Dance students to
            <b>
             <a href="../curriculum/studyabroad.html">
              study abroad.
             </a>
            </b>
            More than half of our students spend a semester in one of several excellent programs specifically designed to complement the curriculum.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            Many students head to the
            <b>
             <a href="http://www.dell-arte.org/" target="_blank">
              Accademia dell'Arte
             </a>
             , in Arezzo, Italy,
            </b>
            which offers intense conservatory training in both theatre and dance, with highly respected teachers. (And no, you don't need to know any Italian!)
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            Others head to programs at
            <b>
             <a href="http://www.gold.ac.uk/" target="_blank">
              Goldsmiths College
             </a>
            </b>
            and
            <b>
             <a href="http://www.qmul.ac.uk/" target="_blank">
              Queen Mary College,
             </a>
            </b>
            University of London, to study alongside students from across England and around the world, and to immerse themselves in
            <b>
             London's rich cultural life.
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size:150%; color:#7a0109; margin-bottom:8px; margin-top:18px;">
            <b>
             A Track Record of Success
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom:0px;">
            <b>
             Our graduates are prepared
            </b>
            for study at the graduate level, pursue lives as professional artists, and contribute creative leadership in many other careers.
            <b>
             <a href="../meetouralumni/index.html">
              Muhlenberg alumni
             </a>
             are working
            </b>
            as actors, directors, designers, choreographers, dancers, stage managers and technicians, educators, and arts administrators on Broadway, in film and television, and in major regional and educational theatres across the nation.
           </p>
