          <img src="/images/main/academics/theatre-dance/ampersand.gif" align="right" alt="&amp;" />
          <p style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 28px; margin-right:195px;">
           <nobr>
            <span style="color:#cc092f; font-size:130%;">
             <strong>
              <i>
               And
              </i>
              is what we do.
             </strong>
            </span>
           </nobr>
           It's the spirit of Muhlenberg Theatre &amp; Dance &mdash; a philosophy, a strategy, a technique.
           <nobr>
            <span style="color:#cc092f; font-size:130%;">
             <strong>
              <i>
               And
              </i>
              is collaboration:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </nobr>
           director and choreographer, students and faculty, technicians and actors, designers and carpenters, the chorus and the star. And is finding your place in the whole, making your contribution.
           <nobr>
            <span style="color:#cc092f; font-size:130%;">
             <strong>
              <i>
               And
              </i>
              is synergy:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </nobr>
           theatre and dance, Broadway and avant-garde, action and theory, principle and practice, jazz hands and pathos. It's finding the whole that exceeds the sum. It's making connections.
           <nobr>
            <span style="color:#cc092f; font-size:130%;">
             <strong>
              <i>
               And
              </i>
              is accomplishment:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </nobr>
           artistic excellence and student experience, national acclaim and independence of spirit.
           <nobr>
            <span style="color:#cc092f; font-size:130%;">
             <strong>
              <i>
               And
              </i>
              is education:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </nobr>
           knowledge and technique, ideas and work, performing arts and the liberal arts, history and innovation, breadth and depth.
           <nobr>
            <strong>
             <span style="color:#cc092f; font-size:130%;">
              <i>
               And
              </i>
              is art:
             </span>
            </strong>
           </nobr>
           the darkness and the spotlight. The audience and the artist.
           <nobr>
            Us and you.
            <span style="color:#cc092f; font-size:130%;">
             <b>
              Join us.
             </b>
            </span>
           </nobr>
          </p>
