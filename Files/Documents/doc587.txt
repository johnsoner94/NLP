          <h1>
          </h1>
          <div class="add07">
           <div class="content-add10">
            <strong>
             <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="Title" -->
             <!-- InstanceEndEditable -->
            </strong>
            <span>
             <strong>
              Post 9/11 GI Bill
             </strong>
             <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="MainContent" -->
            </span>
            <p>
             <span>
              It&rsquo;s Your Turn Now&hellip; The
              <strong>
               Wescoe School
              </strong>
              is proud to support military personnel and veterans seeking to continue their education.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p align="left">
             <span>
              If you&rsquo;re browsing this site, you&rsquo;re taking the first important step in making use of the benefits entitled to you as a United States service person. The academic advisors at the
              <strong>
               Wescoe School
              </strong>
              &nbsp;look forward to welcoming you to our campus.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p align="left">
             <strong>
              <span class="style20">
               Yellow Ribbon Program
              </span>
             </strong>
             <strong>
              <span class="style20">
               at Muhlenberg College
               <img src="/media/contentassets/images/wescoe/Yellow_ribbon_001.png" alt="Wescoe School" style="  padding : 2;   float : right;" />
               <br />
               <br />
              </span>
              &zwnj;
             </strong>
             <span class="style20">
              Under the
              <strong>
               <a href="/media/contentassets/pdf/wescoe/TheYellowRibbonGIEducationEnhancementProgram.pdf">
                Yellow Ribbon program,
               </a>
              </strong>
              tuition and fees are fully covered for veterans with 100% eligibility under the GI Bill
              <span class="style5">
               &reg;
              </span>
              .
             </span>
            </p>
            <p align="left">
             Muhlenberg College has partnered with the US Department of Veterans Affairs in offering this scholarship which covers the difference between the cost of tuition and the reimbursed amount provided by the post 9/11 GI Bill, thereby covering 100% of the tuition for eligible veterans.
            </p>
            <p align="left">
             Our academic advisors look forward to welcoming you to our beautiful campus and assisting with your educational goals.&nbsp; We thank you and your family for the sacrifices you have made to serve our country.
             <span class="style20">
              <strong>
               <br />
               <br />
               <a href="http://www.gibill.va.gov/benefits/post_911_gibill/yellow_ribbon_program.html">
                Click Here
               </a>
               for more information.
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <div id="link1">
             <p>
              We are excited to help you navigate the process to explore majors, understand your benefits, register for classes each semester, and be available for advising throughout your entire time with us.
             </p>
             <p>
              If you served on Active Duty, you might be eligible for education benefits offered by the Department of Veterans Affairs. For example, the Post-9/11 GI Bill provides financial support for educational and housing expenses to individuals with at least 90 days of aggregate service after September 10, 2001, or individuals discharged with a a service-connected disability after 30 days. You must have received an honorable discharge to be eligible for the Post-9/11 GI Bill.
             </p>
             <div>
              <p>
               If you are currently serving in the military, you may be eligible for funding offered through the Department of Defense Tuition Assistance program. Check your eligibility status and the amount for which you qualify with your Service prior to enrolling.
              </p>
              <p>
               If you are the spouse or child of a service member who is serving on active duty Title 10 orders in the pay grades of E1-E5, O1-O2, or W1-W2, you may be eligible for financial assistance from the Department of Defense for education, training, and/or the occupational license and credentials necessary for a portable career.
              </p>
              <p>
               If you are the spouse or child of a service member, you may be eligible for transfer of the service member's Post 9/11 GI Bill benefits to you.
              </p>
             </div>
             <p class="style8">
              <strong>
               We suggest the following:&zwnj;
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p class="style7">
              1). Contact the
              <strong>
               Wescoe Schoo
              </strong>
              l office to make a day or evening appointment with one of our advisors: 484-664-3300&nbsp;or&nbsp;
              <a href="mailto:wescoe@muhlenberg.edu">
               wescoe@muhlenberg.edu
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <table align="right" border="0" style="height: 231px; width: 220px;">
              <tbody>

             <p class="style7">
              2).
              <a href="/main/wescoe/prospectivestudents/veteransportal/checklist/">
               Complete the application for the Post 9/11 GI Bill.&nbsp;
              </a>
             </p>
             <p class="style7">
              Together we will plan an academic and/or career path for you.
              <br />
              <br />
              The
              <strong>
               Wescoe School
              </strong>
              provides tuition deferment, as well as book vouchers to our student veterans. Please see your advisor for assistance.
             </p>
             <hr />
             <div>
              <strong>
               <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                A's for Vets
               </span>
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div>
              <p>
               The Wescoe School is a founding member of the A's (Academics) for Vets organization. Our mission is to bring together a group of higher education learning institutions in the Greater Lehigh Valley and collaborate to train, educate, and support discharged military veterans and their qualifying dependents to be successful in achieving their professional and personal educational goals. The vision is that through these efforts all veterans attending these schools will gain meaningful employment and/or achieve their academic goals, preferably in the Greater Lehigh Valley area.
              </p>
             </div>
             <div>
              <p>
               <img src="/media/contentassets/images/wescoe/Asforvets-150x160.png" alt="" style="  padding : 2;   float : left;" />

                Facebook

               page.
              </p>

             <p class="style7">
              <strong>
               Military Leave Policy /&nbsp;
              </strong>
              <strong>
               Readmission for Military Deployment:
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Students on leave&nbsp;for military deployment will be readmitted with the same academic status achieved when last attended.&nbsp;Length of absence not to exceed 5 years.&nbsp;
              <em>
               (Exceptions being Veterans with dishonorable discharge, bad conduct discharge or court martial offenses).
              </em>
             </p>
             <p class="style7">
              The
              <strong>
               Wescoe School
              </strong>
              is here to support your college experience every step of the way. We recognize the important contributions and sacrifices you and your family have made and now it&rsquo;s your turn to enjoy one of the valuable benefits of your service to our country.
             </p>
             <p class="style7">
              In addition to our academic advising, we invite you to take part in a special course created just for military personnel and/or their spouse at no cost to you.&nbsp; Contact
