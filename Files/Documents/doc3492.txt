            <ul>
             <li>
              In Fall 2012, President Randy Helm agreed to fund an incentive program to encourage employee home ownership in the Allentown School district, to help offset closing costs for employees who buy homes in the Allentown School District. The College will offer a maximum of five $1,000 stipends per year. The stipulation is that the employee purchase a primary residential home in the Allentown School District. The employee must not currently own a primary residence in the Allentown School District. President Helm has agreed to fund this project for five years. At that point, it will be evaluated to see if it has been well utilized. Please use the&nbsp;
