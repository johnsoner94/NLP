        <h2 style="color:#333333; margin-bottom:20px;">
         How to Get to Muhlenberg
        </h2>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="custom-content">
       <p>
        <img src="/media/kdmedia/images/radius-graphic.jpg" alt="" style="  padding : 10px; width:300px;;   float : right;" />
        Muhlenberg College is closer than you think. Just 89 miles west of New York City, 60 miles north of Philadelphia, 181 miles north of Washington, DC, and 310 miles south west of metropolitan Boston, Muhlenberg College welcomes your visit
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         By Car:
         <br />
        </strong>
        If using a GPS, please enter this address:
        <br />
        2400 West Chew Street
        <br />
        Allentown, PA 18104
       </p>
       <p>
        Follow Interstate Route 78 (east-west) or I-476 (Northeast Extension of the Pennsylvania Turnpike) to U.S. Route 22, then U.S. Route 22 or I-78 to Allentown exits. From 22, take Cedar Crest Boulevard exit south (left) to third traffic light, take a left on Chew Street, and follow Chew Street about one mile to the campus. From 78, take the Cedar Crest Boulevard exit north (right) and go six traffic lights. Take a right on to Chew Street and follow W. Chew Street about one mile to the campus.
        <br />
        <br />
        <strong>
         By Air:
        </strong>
        <br />
        Lehigh Valley International Airport is located 15 minutes by car from the Muhlenberg campus. The airport is served by American, Continental, Delta, Northwest, United and US Airways, as well as their "express" extensions. For addtional information contact the Lihigh Valley International Airport at 800-FLY-LVIA (800-359-5842).
        <br />
        <br />
        <strong>
         By Bus:
         <br />
        </strong>
        Beiber, Greyhound-Trailways, and Trans Bridge Lines have depot facilities in downtown Allentown. Bus service runs from JFK, LaGuardia and Newark airports to Allentown. Newark is closest.
        <br />
        <br />
        <em>
         Allentown Bus Terminal
        </em>
        <br />
        325 W Hamilton St
        <br />
        Allentown, PA 18101
        <br />
        610-434-6188
        <br />
        <br />
        <em>
         Carl R. Bieber Tourways
        </em>
        <br />
        4440 Hamilton Blvd.
        <br />
        Allentown, PA
       </p>
