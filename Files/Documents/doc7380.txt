          <h2>
           Activating the NYTimes.com Pass
          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="roundbox innerbox innerbox_1" id="content15116894">
          <p>
           <img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="http://lgimages.s3.amazonaws.com/data/imagemanager/63912/the_new_york_times_logo.png" alt="New York Times" height="73" width="496" />
          </p>
          <p>
           Trexler Library is happy to offer a NYTimes.com Academic Site License providing full access to news, multimedia, and archives of the New York Times. &nbsp;This access is only available to current Muhlenberg College faculty, students, and staff.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <b>
            To ACTIVATE your PASS:
           </b>
          </h2>
          <ul>
           <li>
            While on campus, connected to our campus network, go to
            <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/grouppass">
             http://www.nytimes.com/grouppass
            </a>
            .
           </li>
           <li>
            Using your
            <b>
             Muhlenberg email address
            </b>
            , click on &lsquo;Create an Account&rsquo; or &lsquo;Log in to Continue&rsquo; if you already have an account.
           </li>
           <li>
            If activated correctly, you should see a screen indicating the expiration of your pass (should be about a year later).
           </li>
           <li>
            Once activated, you may now access NYTimes.com from any device, from any location (using your email address as the username and the password you created for this NYTimes pass).
           </li>
          </ul>
          <h2>
           <b>
            Problems?
           </b>
          </h2>
          <ul>
           <li>
            The Group Pass must be activated while physically within our campus network.
           </li>
           <li>
            After activation, you must be logged in to your NYTimes.com account which is associated with your Group Pass registration (Muhlenberg email address)
           </li>
           <li>
            If you a current NYTimes Digital subscriber (using your Muhlenberg email), you will not be able to claim a pass.&nbsp;
           </li>
           <li>
            If you have a daily pass, that pass must expire before claim this year long pass.
           </li>
           <li>
            Other issues, contact Tina Hertel (
            <a href="mailto:thertel@muhlenberg.edu">
             thertel@muhlenberg.edu
            </a>
            ) or x3550.
           </li>
          </ul>

           </li>
