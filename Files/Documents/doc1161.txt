     The Martin Art Gallery manages the College’s collection of 1,600 European and American works on paper from the Florence Foerderer Tonner Collection, more than 700 photogravures from Edward S. Curtis’s
     <i>
      The North American Indian
     </i>
     , and a rich and growing body of contemporary works of art gifted largely by alumni. The Florence Foerderer Tonner Collection and the Edward S. Curtis Collection are presently available.
