          <h1>
          </h1>
          <div class="content-add05">
           <h3>
            Internships
           </h3>
           <p>
            <strong>
             ALL INTERNSHIPS MUST BE REGISTERED WITH THE REGISTRAR&rsquo;S OFFICE.&nbsp;
            </strong>
            &nbsp;
            <strong>
             <span class="style1">
              DEADLINE FOR THE FALL 2014 SEMESTER IS FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 12, 2014.&nbsp;
             </span>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p class="style6">
            <strong>
             <span class="italic style3">
              REFER TO THE INTERNSHIP MANUAL:
             </span>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="/media/migration-feb10094351/main/academics/healthprofessions/students/FINALINTERNSHIPMANUAL-REVISEDSEPTEMBER2013-PDF.pdf">
             http://www.muhlenberg.edu/pdf/main/aboutus/careercenter/internship_manual.pdf
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             <strong>
              SITE SUPERVISOR MANUAL:
             </strong>
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p align="center">
            <strong>
             <em>
              ALL FORMS FOUND IN THE INTERNSHIP AND SITE SUPERVISOR MANUAL MUST BE SIGNED AND DISTRIBUTED TO THE CORRECT PEOPLE.
             </em>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="italic">
             A journal must be kept for all internships and submitted to your Faculty Advisor, Cailin Pachter, and the Site Supervisor when the internship is complete.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p align="center">
            <strong>
             <span class="style7">
              TRANSPORTATION TO AND FROM AN INTERNSHIP IS THE STUDENT'S&nbsp; RESPONSIBILITY.&nbsp;
             </span>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p class="style5">
            <strong>
             LEHIGH VALLEY HEALTH NETWORK
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Lehigh Valley Health Network offers a Research/Scholar program.&nbsp; Research scholars gain educational experience within their departments under the supervision of a mentor.&nbsp; You must have completed at least one year of college to apply.
           </p>
           <p>
            For more information on this program go to:
            <br />
            <a href="http://www.lvhn.org/research_and_education/college_student_programs/research_scholar_program">
             http://www.lvhn.org/research_and_education/college_student_programs/research_scholar_program
            </a>
            &nbsp;
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             LEHIGH
            </strong>
            <strong>
             VALLEY HEALTH&nbsp; NETWORK/PHOEBE NURSING HOME
             <br />
             LIFE REVIEW PROGRAM
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            This internship is collaborate with Lehigh Valley Division of Geriatrics, Phoebe Nursing Home and Muhlenberg College.&nbsp; Students each semester are trained to visit with the elderly residents and conduct a Life Review.&nbsp; Students will develop communication and listening skills and gain an understanding of complications of caring for the elderly within our societies' current health system. &nbsp;They will also visit the Alzheimer's Unit.&nbsp;
           </p>
           <p>
            Students will be required to visit the Nursing Home patients twice weekly and reminisce and provide companionship with them.&nbsp; Attending Educational Conferences will be required as well.&nbsp; This internship earns a .5 unit of credit.
           </p>
           <p>
            Contact the Pre-Professional Office for further information.
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             PHOEBE HOME - OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Contact the Pre-Professional Office for further information.
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             ST. LUKE'S HOSPITAL
             <br />
             PRE-MED OBSERVER PROGRAM
             <br />
             Bethlehem and Allentown
             <br />
             <br />
            </strong>
            The Pre-Med Observer Program is designed to give exemplary students an opportunity to preview medicine for approximately twelve weeks through observations with residents, medical students, and staff physicians.&nbsp;
            <br />
            Areas covered included Podiatry, Family Health Clinic, OB/GYN, Emergency Department, and Surgery.&nbsp;
           </p>
           <p>
            Students are requested to visit twelve different diagnostic treatment and support areas to understand the roll they play in the patient's care.&nbsp;You will be required to attend Tuesday morning presentations (7:30-9:00 a.m.).&nbsp; It is hoped this broad exposure will give you a realistic view of the profession.
           </p>
           <p>
            You will observe a minimum of 60 hours and will be awarded .5 unit of credit for this internship.&nbsp; This will be graded P/F.&nbsp; Applicants must be of junior status with a minimum 3.0 GPA to be considered for this program.&nbsp;
           </p>
           <p>
            Contact the Pre-Professional Office for further information.
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             ST. LUKE'S HOSPITAL
             <br />
            </strong>
            <strong>
             PHYSICIAN ASSISTANT SHADOW PROGRAM
            </strong>
            <br />
            <strong>
             Bethlehem, PA
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Tuesday morning presentations (7:30-9:00 a.m.) are mandatory.&nbsp; Your schedule must permit at least one morning a week from 7-11 observation and one afternoon a week.The program is designed to give you an opportunity to observe the Physician Assistant in the physician's office, surgery, the clinic, and obstetrical areas prior to making a commitment to the profession.&nbsp;
           </p>
           <p>
            Applicants must be at least a sophomore with a 3.25 GPA to be considered.&nbsp;
           </p>
           <p>
            Contact the Pre-Professional Office for further information.
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             ST. LUKE&rsquo;S HOSPITAL
             <br />
             DEPARTMENT OF COMMUNITY HEALTH
            </strong>
            <br />
            <strong>
             Bethlehem, PA
            </strong>
            - This internship is reserved for Public Health minors.&nbsp; If there is availability, other health profession students can apply.
           </p>
           <p>
            St. Luke&rsquo;s Hospital, Department of Community Health offers three different areas of medicine.
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Students who are HIV/STD trained.
            </li>
            <li>
             Students interested in other areas of public health with a pre-med and/or pre-dental focus.
            </li>
            <li>
             Students who are interested in developing new programs, including obesity prevention, chronic disease prevention, heart disease, cancer, diabetes.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            An orientation must be attended prior to the start of the internship.&nbsp; A schedule of internship hours will be implemented for the semester.&nbsp; This internship can be for either .5 unit of credit (5/6 hours a week) or 1 unit of credit (9-12 hours a week).
           </p>
           <p>
            Contact the Pre-Professional Office for further information.
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             GOOD SHEPHERD - PHYSICAL THERAPY&nbsp;
            </strong>
            &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
           </p>
           <p>
            Good Shepherd Rehabilitation Hospital is a world-class rehabilitation facility.&nbsp; They offer the student an internship in physical therapy.
           </p>
           <p>
            Contact the Pre-Professional Office for further information.
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             LEHIGH VALLEY HEALTH NETWORK - DENTAL INTERNSHIP
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Please contact the Pre-Professional Office for further information. &nbsp;
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             PODIATRY INTERNSHIP
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             TEMPLE UNIVERSITY WINTER AND SUMMER INTERNSHIP PROGRAMS
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p align="left">
            Interns will attend workshops and seminars on podiatric medicine, shadow and work with podiatric physicians in the hospital, private practice and outpatient clinic settings, and work with a TUSPM student-doctor throughout the internship on a case presentation.&nbsp;
           </p>
           <p align="left">
            For further information, please visit
            <a href="http://podiatry.temple.edu">
             http://podiatry.temple.edu
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             OPTOMETRY INTERNSHIP
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Contact the Pre-Professional Office for further information.
           </p>
           <hr />
           <p>
            <strong>
             LEHIGH VALLEY ZOO
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Internships are available at the Lehigh Valley Zoo.&nbsp; An application can be found at
            <br />
            <a href="http://www.lvzoo.org/jobs_internships.cfm">
             http://www.lvzoo.org/jobs_internships.cfm.
            </a>
           </p>
