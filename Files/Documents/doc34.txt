       <div class="description pull-right">
        <h3>
         Meet with an Admissions Counselor
        </h3>
        <p>
         A Personal Interview is highly recommended for first-year applicants: It gives us a chance to get a better understanding of your talents and plans. A personal interview is required for transfer applicants, those applying under our SAT-optional program, and candidates for our cooperative programs, including the&nbsp;
         <a href="/main/admissions/physicalandoccupationaltherapy/">
          3-3 Jefferson PT or OT Programs
         </a>
         , the&nbsp;
         <a href="/main/admissions/optometry/">
          SUNY College of Optometry Program
         </a>
         &nbsp;and the&nbsp;
         <a href="/main/admissions/dentistry/">
          3-4 U Penn Bio Dental Program
         </a>
         .
         <br />
         You'll talk with an admissions counselor for about 30-45 minutes.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Juniors can Interview beginning in the middle of March of their junior year
         </li>
         <li>
          Seniors can interview through Feb. 15 of their senior year
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="/main/admissions/areainterviews/">
           Interview in your hometown
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
       </div>

        <h3>
         Attend an info session
        </h3>
        <p>
         These one-hour group sessions offer an introduction to Muhlenberg and our admissions process. You'll get the chance to ask questions and meet other prospective students. It's a great way to take a "first look" before you decide to interview.
        </p>

        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="/main/admissions/campusdayvisitprogram/">
          On a day visit
         </a>
         , you'll attend a class (or two) and have lunch with a student host - a great way to see what we mean when we say that Muhlenberg is a place of passionate teaching and incomparable community. Day visits are scheduled for high school seniors at least two weeks in advance and are subject to host availability.
        </p>
