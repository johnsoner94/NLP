       <div class="col-xs-12">
        <h2 style="color:#333333; margin-bottom:20px;">
         Housing Accommodations
        </h2>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="custom-content">
       <p>
        Looking for a low cost housing alternative for your summer internship program? Summer corporate housing is available at Muhlenberg College.
       </p>
       <p>
        The College offers a variety of housing styles from traditional residence halls to the Village, which offers an apartment like common center with four private residences.
       </p>
       <p>
        Contact JoEllen Rooney at
        <a href="tel:4846643810">
         484-664-3810
        </a>
        or
        <a href="mailto:jrooney@muhlenberg.edu">
         jrooney@muhlenberg.edu
        </a>
        for additional details.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img src="/media/contentassets/images/about/events/VillageInteriorLivingRoom-s-485x260.jpg" alt="" style="     " />
       </p>
       <p>
        Linen services can also be provided upon request.
       </p>
       <p>
        For our summer conference program, Residential Services can accomodate individuals in the traditional dorm like setting or in private residence style suites.
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Muhlenberg College is now accepting intern housing payments through a third party vendor called Tuition Management Services or TMS. Payments can be made through the payment link via two methods:
         <br />
         <br />
         1. Payments are accepted by Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) from a checking or statement savings account at no charge.
         <br />
         2. Payments are accepted by credit card payment. Convenience fees between 2-4% will be charged for this service.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Checks and money orders may still be mailed in for payments to the address on the invoice. Cash payments will only be accepted at the Controller's Office during business hours Monday through Thursday.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Be prepared to provide the invoice number which you are paying, bank account number, bank routing number (or credit card number) and amount when making a one-time payment.
        </em>
       </p>
