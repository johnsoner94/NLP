         <div align="justify">
          <p class="body">
           Through the wisdom, dedication, and hard work of those who have gone before us, Muhlenberg has achieved a distinguished reputation among America's undergraduate liberal arts institutions.&nbsp; In the fall of 2003, we, the current stewards of this remarkable place, turned our thoughts to the College's future as we met to formulate a Strategic Plan for the coming decade.&nbsp; Planning required us to think deeply and creatively about the challenges that face our college, our society, and our world, and the role Muhlenberg will play in preparing young men and women to meet those challenges.&nbsp;
          </p>
          <div class="body">
          </div>
          <div class="body">
           Muhlenberg's strategic planning process engaged Trustees, faculty, staff, students, alumni, parents, and other members of the community in charting our course for the next decade.&nbsp; Because our resources are limited, we need to make careful choices.&nbsp; Because we can only achieve ambitions worthy of this great institution if we work together, our strategic plan represents a shared and aspirational vision of our common future.&nbsp;&nbsp;
          </div>
          <div class="body">
           &nbsp;
          </div>
          <div class="body">
           This website provides information about this process, its participants and goals, and the Strategic Plan approved by the Board of Trustees on October 23, 2004.&nbsp;
