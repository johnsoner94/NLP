     <div class="titlerow" style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      Core Databases
     </div>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Business Source Complete
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=bth">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&defaultdb;=bth
       </a>
       <br />
       Available by library subscription. Largely full text database of scholarly and trade journals covering all aspects of accounting, business, and economics. Full text journals include, among many others, the Harvard Business Review (1985-present, including all cases originally published in the Review), Academy of Management Journal, Accounting Review, and Journal of Marketing. Also includes several hundred in-depth reports on publicly traded companies. To access, click on the Company Profiles tab at the top of Business Source Complete.
      </li>
      <li>
       Business Week
       <br />
       <a href="http://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.aspx?direct=true&amp;db=bsh&amp;jid=B7OA&amp;site=ehost-live">
        http://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.aspx?direct=true&db;=bsh&jid;=B7OA&site;=ehost-live
       </a>
       <br />
       Top-flight business periodical.
      </li>
      <li>
       EconLit
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.proquest.com/econlitshell/ip?accountid=40980">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.proquest.com/econlitshell/ip?accountid=40980
       </a>
       <br />
       Index to scholarly publications in economics. Links out to other library databases, and to interlibrary loan.
      </li>
      <li>
       JSTOR
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.jstor.org/">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.jstor.org/
       </a>
       <br />
       Full text of peer-reviewed journals from most academic disciplines. Coverage is from the inception of the journal through three to five years ago. Well over 400 journals specific to the study of business and economics.
      </li>
      <li>
       IntelliConnect (CCH)
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://intelliconnect.cch.com/scion/auth.jsp?disableAutoLogin=true&amp;wksso=false">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://intelliconnect.cch.com/scion/auth.jsp?disableAutoLogin=true&wksso;=false
       </a>
       <br />
       Users must possess a valid Muhlenberg email address, and create an account. IntelliConnect provides unlimited access to federal and state tax information plus explanations, analysis and practice aids. Includes daily tax news, FASB materials, as well as GAAP and GAAS guides.
       <a href="http://libapp.muhlenberg.edu/cannon/Intelliconnect.pdf">
        User guide
       </a>
       .
       <br />
      </li>
     </ul>
