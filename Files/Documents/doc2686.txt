     <div class="titlerow" style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      Having trouble finding what you need from Encompass Search? Try:
     </div>
     <div style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      <ul>
       <li>
        Place titles or other phrases in quotation marks to search your exact phrase (e.g.,
        <em>
         "catcher in the rye"
        </em>
        ).
        <br />
        <br />
       </li>
       <li>
        If you get too many results, try adding the author's name in your revised search (e.g.,
        <em>
         "complete poems" AND "emily dickinson"
        </em>
        ).
        <br />
        <br />
       </li>
       <li>
        Capitalize AND and OR to distinguish these search term connectors from your search terms (e.g.,
        <em>
         heart OR cardiac
        </em>
        ).
       </li>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Use AND to search for items that contain both of your terms.
        </li>
        <li>
         Use OR to search for items that contain either of your terms.
         <br />
         <br />
        </li>
       </ul>
       <li>
        Limit results by clicking the checkboxes on the left sidebar of your search results (e.g., choose
        <em>
         DVD
        </em>
        on the left if you want the DVD format of Jane Austen's
        <em>
         Pride and Prejudice
        </em>
        ).
        <br />
        <br />
       </li>
       <li>
        Items available to Muhlenberg College users are labeled
        <em>
         Held by: Muhlenberg College Trexler Library
        </em>
        . Items without a
        <em>
         Held by
        </em>
        label are not in Muhlenberg College's collection.
        <br />
        <br />
       </li>
       <li>
        Most items not owned by Muhlenberg College can be requested on interlibrary loan. Click on the
        <em>
         Request Item
        </em>
        button.
       </li>
      </ul>
     </div>
