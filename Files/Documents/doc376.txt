           <p align="justify">
            <span class="body">
             As co-chairs of the Middle-States Self-Study Steering Committee, we welcome you to this website. This is where you will find an overview of the process, Self-Study personnel, documents generated by Committee and Task Force work, and minutes from Steering Committee meetings.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p align="justify" class="body">
            The College has been accredited by the Middle States Commission in Higher Education, a division of the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools, since 1921. We will undergo our formal review in spring, 2006; however, work has been progressing on the Self-Study since fall, 2003, when the Steering Committee and Task force members were appointed.
           </p>
           <p align="justify" class="body">
            Muhlenberg College is perfectly poised for the opportunity to engage in its ten-year Middle States reaccredidation review. President Helm has set the tone for a culture of assessment and planning through the strategic planning process. Consistent with this tone and process, the College has chosen the Comprehensive model of self-study. This model complements the Strategic Planning process, although the structure, timetable and some personnel vary. Both enterprises are committed to consistent institution-wide assessment and open collaboration. Both enterprises are committed to seeing what the College does well (and why) and to determining what the College can do more effectively (and how).
           </p>
           <p align="justify" class="body">
            As Co-Chairs of the Steering Committee, we hope that members of the Muhlenberg community will participate where appropriate in the review and use this website to become engaged in the Self-Study process as it unfolds over the next eighteen months.
           </p>
           <p align="justify" class="body">
            Cordially,
            <br />
            Kathleen Harring Carol Shiner Wilson
           </p>
