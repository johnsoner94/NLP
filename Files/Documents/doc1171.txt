          <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 5px;">
           <span class="fsl">
            A spectacular evening of ballet, contemporary dance, 
tap, and jazz, Master Choreographers showcases exciting new dance works 
choreographed by nationally and internationally acclaimed guest artists 
and faculty.
           </span>
           <br />
          </p>
          <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 5px;">
           <span class="fsl">
            This season's Master Choreographers concert will 
feature restagings of major works by renowned choreographers Karole 
Armitage, Donald McKayle, and Shapiro &amp; Smith. The evening will also
 feature world premiere works by Heidi Cruz-Austin, alumna of the 
Pennsylvania Ballet; Dance Program chair Karen Dearborn; Jeffrey 
Peterson, former dancer with Danny Buraczeski's JAZZDANCE; and Shelley 
Oliver, director of Shelley Oliver Tap Dancers.
           </span>
          </p>
