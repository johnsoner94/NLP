          <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="EditMainContent" -->
          <div class="content-add05">
           <strong>
            Student Training for Online Registration
           </strong>
           <p>
            The instructions below are meant to assist you with the process of registering online. &nbsp;If you have any questions, however, please feel free to ask. &nbsp;We are here to help!
           </p>
           <br />
           <ol style="margin: 0 0 0 25px;">
            <li>
             Go to the Registrar&rsquo;s Web Site. (
             <a href="/main/aboutus/registrar/">
              http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/aboutus/registrar/
             </a>
             )
            </li>
            <li>
             In the right hand menu move your mouse to Student Info (CapStone). &nbsp;Click on Login.
            </li>
            <li>
             Click on the button for &ldquo;Student Access&rdquo;.
            </li>
            <li>
             Enter your BergID (009999999); date of birth (mm/dd/yyyy); and your password. &nbsp;Use your mouse to click the &ldquo;Sign On&rdquo; button. &nbsp;If you do not remember your password, come to the Registrar&rsquo;s Office and we will help you.
            </li>
            <li>
             You now have two registration methods you may use:
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Registration Form:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <br />
           <ul>
            <li>
             If you know the courses for which you would like to register, click on Registration.
            </li>
            <li>
             If this is your first time through, read &ldquo;Who Can Register Using the Internet&rdquo;. &nbsp;If this is not your first time, through, close the window.
            </li>
            <li>
             The Select Term will be set to Fall 2016.
            </li>
            <li>
             Enter your password again.
            </li>
            <li>
             Enter the courses for which you would like to register (ex: ACT10101).
            </li>
            <li>
             Choose Add.
            </li>
            <li>
             Click on the &ldquo;Click Here to Register&rdquo; button.
            </li>
            <li>
             It will ask you to Confirm your Intent to Register (in other words, do you really want to register). &nbsp;Click OK.
            </li>
            <li>
             At the top of the page you will receive a message. &nbsp;It will either say that your registration was successful or give you an error message.
            </li>
            <li>
             If there is a problem with one of the courses, remove it from the Registration form and add the rest. &nbsp;Then go back to fix the problem.
            </li>
            <li>
             If you were successful, you may add other courses, or if you are done, print out your schedule, and sign off.
            </li>
            <li>
             If you received an error message, determine what you need to do to fix the problem.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <strong>
             closed course
            </strong>
            <strong>
             :
            </strong>
            course is full; choose another course or see the instructor to request online permission to register for the course
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             course conflict:
            </strong>
            two of the courses on your registration form overlap in time; choose another course or see the instructor to request online permission to register regardless of the time conflict
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             missing pre/co-
            </strong>
            <strong>
             requisite:
            </strong>
            you have not taken or are not taking one of the pre/co-requisites for the course; choose another course or see the instructor to request online permission to take the course without the pre/co-requisite.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             signature requirement:
            </strong>
            for courses requiring permission of the instructor, speak with the instructor and request online permission
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             missing companion course:
            </strong>
            you have not registered for one part of a course (i.e. science lecture but no lab); add the missing course, choose another course, or see the instructor to request online permission to take the course without the companion course.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             too late to add:&nbsp;
            </strong>
            it is too late to add the course on your own; you must request the instructor&rsquo;s permission to add the course.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Course Schedule:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <br />
           <ul>
            <ul>
             <li>
              If you are not sure of the courses for which you want to register, click on Course Schedule.
             </li>
             <li>
              Select the Term (Fall 2016) and a Discipline. &nbsp;It is automatically set to show you the Meeting Information; you may click the boxes by Pre/Co-Requisites and/or Course Description if you would like to see that information as well.
             </li>
             <li>
              Associated with the courses is a check box under Select Course. &nbsp;If you would like to add that course, click on the box.
             </li>
             <li>
              If you change your mind, click the box again and the check mark will disappear.
             </li>
             <li>
              In the middle of the screen will appear a &ldquo;Clear All&rdquo; button, an &ldquo;Open Registration Window&rdquo; button, and a list of the course(s) you have checked.
             </li>
             <li>
              You may click on &ldquo;Open Registration Window&rdquo; and the course(s) you have selected will be automatically entered on the Registration Form.
             </li>
             <li>
              If you are not done, you may choose other courses from that discipline or go to another discipline. &nbsp;All the courses that you choose will remain showing in the middle of the screen.
             </li>
             <li>
              After you have chosen all the courses you want, click on &ldquo;Open Registration Window&rdquo;. The course(s) will be on the Registration Form.
             </li>
             <li>
              If this is your first time through, read &ldquo;Who Can Register Using the Internet&rdquo;. &nbsp;If this is not your first time through, close the window.
             </li>
             <li>
              The Select Term will be set to Fall 2016.
             </li>
             <li>
              Enter your password again.
             </li>
             <li>
              Click on the &ldquo;Click Here to Register&rdquo; button.
             </li>
             <li>
              It will ask you to Confirm your Intent to Register (in other words, do you really want to register). &nbsp;Click OK.
             </li>
             <li>
              At the top of the page you will receive a message. &nbsp;It will either say that your registration was successful or give you an error message.
             </li>
            </ul>
           </ul>
           <br />
           <ul>
            <li>
             If there is a problem with one of the courses, remove it from the Registration form and add the rest. &nbsp;Then go back to fix the problem.
            </li>
            <li>
             If you were successful, you may add other courses, or if you are done, print out your schedule, and sign off.
            </li>
            <li>
             If you received an error message, determine what you need to do to fix the problem:
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <strong>
             closed course
            </strong>
            <strong>
             :
            </strong>
            course is full; choose another course or see the instructor to request online permission to register for the course
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             course conflict:
            </strong>
            two of the courses on your registration form overlap in time; choose another course or see the instructor to request online permission to register regardless of the time conflict
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             missing pre/co-
            </strong>
            <strong>
             requisite:
            </strong>
            you have not taken or are not taking one of the pre/co-requisites for the course; choose another course or see the instructor to request online permission to take the course without the pre/co-requisite.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             signature requirement:
            </strong>
            for courses requiring permission of the instructor, speak with the instructor and request online permission
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             missing companion course:
            </strong>
            you have not registered for one part of a course (i.e. science lecture but no lab); add the missing course, choose another course, or see the instructor to request online permission to take the course without the companion course.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             too late to add:&nbsp;
            </strong>
            it is too late to add the course on your own; you must request the instructor&rsquo;s permission to add the course.
           </p>
           <br />
           <ul>
            <li>
             You may go back and forth between the Course Schedule and the Registration Form as much as you would like
            </li>
            <li>
             Remember to Sign Off when you have finished your registration.
            </li>
            <li>
             You may continue to Add and Drop courses until the Add and Drop Deadlines. For Fall 2016 the Add deadline is Friday, September 2 and the Drop deadline is Wednesday, September 7.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p class="center">
            <strong>
             &nbsp;FUN STUFF
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Optional:
            </strong>
            You may choose the &ldquo;Earliest Start Times&rdquo; and the &ldquo;Latest Start Times&rdquo; that would work in your schedule. &nbsp;Remember, however, that you may need to take courses outside of your &ldquo;preferred&rdquo; time frame if that is when the open courses are being offered.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Day-of-Week:
            </strong>
            By clicking in the boxes next to the days of the week, you may search for courses that meet only on certain days of the week.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             GAR:
            </strong>
            If you need a General Academic Requirement, you may select one of the GARs (i.e. &ldquo;AR&rdquo;), and when you &ldquo;submit&rdquo; you will get a listing of all the courses that meet the &ldquo;AR&rdquo; requirement.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             All Courses:
            </strong>
            Under the All Courses list you may choose to list only those courses that are currently &ldquo;open&rdquo;.
           </p>
           <p class="center">
            &nbsp;
            <strong>
             OTHER IMPORTANT STUFF
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Online Registration is a wonderful tool, but it will not make everything perfect and all problems go away. &nbsp;There will still be issues you will need to address as well as processes you will need to follow in order to register for your classes.
           </p>
           <br />
           <ol style="margin: 0 0 0 25px;">
            <li>
             Your registration is based on your class year and the letters of your last name. &nbsp;The positioning will change each semester.
            </li>
            <li>
             You must see your assigned advisor. &nbsp;Only your official advisor has the ability to approve you for registration.
            </li>
            <li>
             If you have two majors, BOTH of your major advisors must approve you for registration.
            </li>
            <li>
             You will not be able to register if you have any financial holds.
            </li>
            <li>
             Since instructors must give you course permissions on the computer, you will not be able to catch them in the hallway, sidewalk, or dining hall to ask them to override a problem and get you into a course. &nbsp;You will need to make an appointment, call them, or send them an email to speak with them.
            </li>
            <li>
             Courses requiring special forms (i.e. Independent Study, Internships, Music Lessons, Pass/Fail, Audit etc.) will continue to be registered in the Registrar&rsquo;s Office.
            </li>
           </ol>
