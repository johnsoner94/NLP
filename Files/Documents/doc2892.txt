          <p>
           Muhlenberg College's football team was driving toward a huge Centennial Conference victory on Saturday before a wild circus interception derailed the Mules against Johns Hopkins.
          </p>
          <p>
           A pass from quarterback Nick Palladino went into the endzone, off of closely defended receiver Nick Lamb and was scooped by Johns Hopkins defensive back Jack Toner, to preserve the Blue Jays' 30-26 victory.
          </p>
          <p>
           It's Johns Hopkins (4-0, 3-0) 31st straight conference victory. Muhlenberg moves to 3-1, 2-1.
          </p>
