            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;">
             <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: #c00000;">
              What is a Regional Club?
              <br />
              <hr />
              <span xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">
               <br />
              </span>
             </span>
            </h3>
            <a onclick="return false;" target="blank" href="http://muhlenberg.imodules.com/s/1570/images/gid2/editor_documents/mapping_the_mules_-_summer_2012.pdf">
            </a>
            <img alt="" src="/s/1570/images/gid2/editor/alumni_map_13-_small.jpg" style="border: 0px solid;" height="306" width="475" />
            <br />
            <br />
            <strong>
             <span style="font-size: 10px;">
              &nbsp;
Map
              <a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/s/1570/images/gid2/editor_documents/natlpagemapbig.pdf">
               PDF
              </a>
             </span>
            </strong>
            <br />
            <br />
            <a onclick="return false;" target="blank" href="http://muhlenberg.imodules.com/admin/index.aspx?sid=1570&amp;gid=2&amp;control_id=616&amp;content_id=159&amp;page_id=412&amp;mode=content&amp;ininit=1&amp;rcid=1119#">
            </a>
            Driven primarily by volunteer leaders, Muhlenberg's Regional Alumni Clubs seek to keep alumni connected with the College, the Alumni Association and with each other, create a network of ambassadors for the Muhlenberg community and provide volunteer and leadership opportunities.
            <br />
            <br />
            The College and the Alumni Association have teamed up to offer Regional Alumni Clubs in those areas with larger alumni populations.
            <br />
            <br />
            Would you like to help create a Regional Group or otherwise volunteer in your area? Click
            <a href="mailto:bergalum@muhlenberg.edu?subject=Regional%20Alumni%20Club%20Interest">
             <span class="ApplyClass">
              here
             </span>
            </a>
            and find out how you might get more involved!
