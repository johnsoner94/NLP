          <h2>
           Defining Key Terms
          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="roundbox innerbox innerbox_1" id="content10140609">
          <p>
           You may be wondering what we mean when we refer to
           <em>
            scholarly
           </em>
           versus
           <em>
            popular
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Scholarly:
            </strong>
            In-depth, research-based articles, books, and book chapters, written by scholars and experts and intended for other researchers, experts, and students.
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Popular:
            </strong>
            Articles, books, and book chapters written by journalists or professional writers for a general audience in common language (e.g., newspapers and magazines).
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;" class="MsoNormal">
           <em>
            Learn more:
            <a href="http://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/content.php?pid=471100&amp;sid=4047073">
             What is Scholarly?
            </a>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           While we're at it, how about a few more key terms...
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Empirical:
            </strong>
            Based on experiment or observation
            <strong>
             .
             <br />
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Peer-Reviewed:
            </strong>
            A process through which some scholarly articles and book chapters are critically evaluated by experts with in-depth knowledge of the research area before publication in order to ensure the information is valid, credible, well-written, and of high quality.
            <span>
             &nbsp;
            </span>
            <strong>
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Literature:
            </strong>
            Scholarly articles, books, and book chapters published on a particular subject.
           </li>
          </ul>
