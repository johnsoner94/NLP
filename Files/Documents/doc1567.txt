            <h1>
             Ways to Give Now
            </h1>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <br />
            </p>
           </div>
           <!-- &lt;nav id="ContentBreadCrumbs" class="col-md-12 hidden-xs breadCrumbs imodSiteMap"&gt;  &lt;li id="bcFirst"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/s/1570/index.aspx?sid=1570&amp;gid=2"&gt;Home&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="nav" id="Giveto'Berg"&gt;&lt;a &gt;Give to 'Berg&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;  &lt;li class="nav" id="OpportunitiesforImpact"&gt;&lt;a &gt;Opportunities for Impact&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;  &lt;li class="navselected" id="WaystoGiveNow"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/s/1570/index.aspx?sid=1570&amp;gid=2&amp;pgid=636"&gt;Ways to Give Now&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;      &lt;/nav&gt; -->
           <div id="ContentMiddle" class="col-md-12">
            <br />
            <strong>
             <span style="font-size: 18px; color: rgb(192, 0, 0);">
              WAYS TO GIVE
              <em>
               here and
              </em>
              <em>
               now
               <br />
              </em>
             </span>
            </strong>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              Muhlenberg College offers a wide range of opportunities for donors at all levels to make a difference in the quality and future of the College. All, of course, are tax deductible within the limits specified by the IRS code. We would recommend that you consult with an attorney or financial adviser when planning your commitment.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              <br />
              <strong>
               Online or by Credit Card:
               <br />
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              Gifts, pledges and pledge payments may be made with Visa, MasterCard or American Express by calling 1-800-859-2243 or
              <a target="_blank" title="Make A Gift Now!" href="https://www.applyweb.com/public/contribute?s=muhlcont">
               make an online gift now
              </a>
              .&nbsp; Contact Jill Anderson, Assistant Vice President for The Muhlenberg Fund and Communication, at 484-664-4327 or
              <a href="mailto:janderson@muhlenberg.edu">
               janderson@muhlenberg.edu
              </a>
              if you have questions.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              <br />
              <strong>
               Cash or Check:
               <br />
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              Current law allows you to deduct the full amount of your cash gift in the year the gift is made up to an amount equal to 50 percent of your adjusted gross income. Checks should be made payable to Muhlenberg College and sent to 2400 Chew Street, Allentown, Pa. 18104.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              <strong>
               <br />
               Matching Gifts:
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              <strong>
               <br />
              </strong>
              Thousands of companies and corporate foundations match gifts to non-profit institutions such as Muhlenberg College. You can double or even triple the value of your gift and enhance your total toward a leadership level contribution by simply notifying your employer of your gift to Muhlenberg. You may be eligible to submit matching gift claims from both your own and your partner&rsquo;s employer for the same gift. Contact the human resources office at your employer, your partner&rsquo;s employer &ndash; or even your former employer, if you are retired &ndash; to inquire about their matching gift program. Call 1-800-859-2243 for more information.
              <strong>
               <br />
               <br />
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              <strong>
               Gifts of Stock:
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              If you have owned a stock for more than 12 months and it has appreciated in value during that time, you would incur a capital gains tax by selling it. However, if you give that stock to Muhlenberg as a gift, you avoid the capital gains tax and can also claim an income tax deduction for the full fair market value of the gift up to an amount equal to 30 percent of your adjusted gross income. Any unused deduction may be carried forward for up to five years. Call 1-800-859-2243 for more information.
              <strong>
               <br />
               <br />
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              <strong>
               Electronic Transfers of Shares of Publicly Traded Stock and Mutual Funds:
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              Shares of publicly traded companies and mutual funds may be transferred electronically to Muhlenberg College by using the Muhlenberg account at Stifel Nicolaus.
              <br />
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              Remember to instruct your broker to contact Mark Mazer, Director of Advancement Services, at 1-800-859-2243 or
              <a title="Electronic Transfer Information" href="mailto:mmazer@muhlenberg.edu?subject=Electronic Transfers">
               mmazer@muhlenberg.edu
              </a>
              regarding the pending transfer, and to share with us your name, the number of shares, the name of the corporation or mutual fund and the anticipated date of transfer.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              You will need to provide your broker with the DTC (Deposit Trust Company) number and Muhlenberg's account number. At Stifel Nicolaus, the DTC Number is
              <strong>
               0793
              </strong>
              , and the Muhlenberg College Account Number is
              <strong>
               4056-5163
              </strong>
              .&nbsp;&nbsp;Our account representative is Brad Greenawald (610-782-5400). Muhlenberg's tax I.D. number is
              <strong>
               23-1352664
              </strong>
              .&nbsp; Accuracy of DTC and account numbers is valid as of 10/1/13.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              <br />
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13px;">
              <strong>
               Estate Gifts:
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             Membership in Muhlenberg's
             <em>
              Circle of 1848
             </em>
             is open to all alumni, parents, faculty, staff and friends who name the College as a beneficiary in their estate plan or through their will, life insurance policy, trust or retirement plan. Legacy gifts from Circle members have a substantial impact on the quality of learning Muhlenberg offers its students and ensure the continued vitality and development of the College from one generation to the next. Contact Gregory Wilson, Planned Giving &amp; Leadership Gift Officer, at 484-664-3224 or
             <a href="mailto:gwilson@muhlenberg.edu?subject=Estate Gift Questions">
              gwilson@muhlenberg.edu
             </a>
             if you have questions.
            </p>
