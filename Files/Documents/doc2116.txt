           <p style="font-size: 120%">
            <b>
             There are several useful ways you can stay on top of what's going on at Muhlenberg Theatre &amp; Dance. Choose the ones that work best for you.
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 150%; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-top: 24px;">
            <b>
             By mail
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; font-weight: normal;">
            We send a season brochure in late summer for the upcoming school year, and a Summer Music Theatre brochure in the spring.
            <a href="mailinglist.html">
             Sign up to get it!
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 150%; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-top: 24px;">
            <b>
             By e-mail
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; font-weight: normal;">
            We send out an occasional update about upcoming performances. No more than once a week &mdash; usually less. (We've been known to throw some discounts out there for our e-mail subscribers...)
            <a href="mailinglist.html">
             Sign up.
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 150%; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-top: 24px;">
            <b>
             On Facebook
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; font-weight: normal;">
            Here's where we post our photos, casual videos, events, notices, and idle chatter. Join in!
            <a href="http://www.facebook.com/muhlenbergtheatredance" target="_blank">
             Check it out.
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 150%; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-top: 24px;">
            <b>
             With Twitter
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; font-weight: normal;">
            Find out what's going on in the Department, 140 characters at a time. We tweet at Muhl_Thea_Dance.
            <a href="http://www.twitter.com/muhl_thea_dance" target="_blank">
             Follow us!
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 150%; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-top: 24px;">
            <b>
             What else?
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px; font-weight: normal;">
            <a href="contact.html">

             nice...
