       <td valign="top">
        <h3 align="center">
         &nbsp;
        </h3>
        <p align="center" class="body">
         <strong>
          Ultimate Goal:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p align="justify" class="body">
         At the end of the first decade of the twenty-first century, Muhlenberg will be nationally acknowledged as a distinguished, intellectually rigorous private undergraduate institution that integrates liberal arts with pre-professional studies in the context of a supportive, diverse community. The Muhlenberg experience will reinforce the importance of character and ethical decision-making, the beneficial power of spiritual life within a variety of faith traditions, and the obligation of graduates to lead lives of leadership and service.
        </p>
        <p align="justify" class="body">
         &nbsp;
        </p>
        <p align="center" class="body">
         <strong>
          Strategic Goals:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ol class="body">
         <li>
          <div align="justify">
           Strengthen the academic program through significant, targeted investments in faculty, staff, and facilities that build upon existing strengths and capitalize on the most promising opportunities for further development of the curriculum within the parameters of our mission statement, fundamental values, and planning principles .
           <sup>
            1
           </sup>
           <br />
           <br />
          </div>
         </li>
         <li>
          <div align="justify">
           Enrich the Muhlenberg experience by further developing and more closely integrating co-curricular and extra-curricular programs with intellectual and curricular life.
           <br />
           <br />
          </div>
         </li>
         <li>
          <div align="justify">
           Further develop Muhlenberg's distinctive identity as a caring community focused on the development of character, individual potential, and engaged citizenship.
           <br />
           <br />
          </div>
         </li>
         <li>
          <div align="justify">
           Support the integration of the liberal arts with selected areas of pre-professional study and leadership development by expanding opportunities for service learning, internships, and international education.
           <br />
           <br />
          </div>
         </li>
         <li>
          <div align="justify">
           Position Muhlenberg as an increasingly attractive option for gifted college-bound students from diverse backgrounds by increasing financial aid resources, strengthening co-curricular programs, and utilizing the unique advantages of our location
           <br />
           <br />
          </div>
         </li>
         <li>
          <div align="justify">
           Significantly strengthen the financial position of the College through careful management of debt, superior investment performance, endowment growth, aggressive and successful expansion of fund-raising efforts, and the development of stronger relationships with constituents.
          </div>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <hr />
        <font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
         <sup>
          1
         </sup>
        </font>
        <span class="footnote">
         These three documents are posted on the Strategic Planning Website and will be referenced in the final draft of the plan.
