           <p style="font-size: 150%">
            <b>
             Get Here By Car
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px;">
            Follow Interstate 78 (east-west), Interstate 476 (Northeast Extension of the Pennsylvania Turnpike), or U.S. Route 22 to 

Allentown, getting off at Cedar Crest Boulevard.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px;">
            From Route 22, take Cedar Crest Boulevard exit south (left) to third traffic light, take a left on Chew Street, and follow Chew Street about one mile to the campus.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px;">
            From I-78, take the Cedar Crest Boulevard exit north (right) and go six traffic lights.  Take a right onto Chew Street and follow Chew Street about one mile to the campus.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px;">
            The College is at 2400 Chew Street, Allentown, between 23rd and 26th Streets.
           </p>
           <span class="more" style="margin-left: 12px;">
            <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/aboutus/tour/driving.html" target="_blank">
             See detailed driving directions and map.
            </a>
           </span>
           <p style="font-size: 150%">
            <b>
             Get Here By Air
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px;">
            Lehigh Valley International Airport is located 15 minutes by car from the Muhlenberg campus. The airport is served by American, Continental, Delta, Northwest, United and US Airways, as well as their "express" extensions. For more information, check the
            <a href="http://www.lvia.org/" target="_blank">
             LVIA website
            </a>
            or call 800-FLY-LVIA (800-359-5842).
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 150%">
            <b>
             Get Here By Bus
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px;">
            Bieber, Greyhound, and Trans-Bridge Lines have depot facilities in Allentown. Bus service runs from New York, Philadelphia, Washington, D.C., northern and western Pennsylvania, and most major cities.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px;">
            <a href="http://www.biebertourways.com" target="_blank">
             Bieber Tourways
            </a>
            <br />
            <a href="http://www.greyhound.com" target="_blank">
             Greyhound
            </a>
            <br />
            <a href="http://www.transbridgelines.com" target="_blank">
             Trans-Bridge
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 12px;">
            Allentown Bus Terminal
            : 610-434-6188
            <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?hl=en&amp;safe=off&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;channel=s&amp;q=allentown+bus+terminal&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;hq=Allentown+Bus+Terminal&amp;hnear=Allentown+Bus+Terminal,+325+W+Hamilton+St,+Allentown,+Pennsylvania+18101-1808&amp;ll=40.610695,-75.462599&amp;spn=0.031602,0.054674&amp;z=14&amp;iwloc=A" target="_blank">
