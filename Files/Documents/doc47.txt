      <div class="page-header" style='background-image:url("/media/kdmedia/images/page-headers/common-questions.jpg");'>
       <h1>
        Common Questions
       </h1>
       <p>
        At Muhlenberg, we welcome questions and we look forward to getting to know you.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="cta-section hidden-sm hidden-md hidden-lg" data-sidebar-buttons-target="data-sidebar-buttons-target">
      </div>
      <div class="custom-content">
       <p>
        For starters, though, we've put together answers to the most common questions. Don't see your question addressed below? Contact us at
        <a class="red" href="/">
         admissions@muhlenberg.edu
        </a>
        to submit a question.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="sidebar-links collapsible-accordian">
       <div class="panel-group " id="collapse-accordion" role="tablist" aria-multiselectable="false">
        <!-- navigation object : Collapsible Accordion Content -->
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#where-is-muhlenberg2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="where-is-muhlenberg2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="where-is-muhlenberg">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">
            Where is Muhlenberg?
           </p>
           <p class="panel-title pull-right">
            <span class="fa fa-plus-square-o">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
           </div>
          </div>
         </a>
         <div id="where-is-muhlenberg2" class="panel-collapse collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="where-is-muhlenberg">
          <div class="panel-body">
           <p>
            Muhlenberg's beautiful campus is nestled in the West End neighborhood of Allentown, PA, in Pennsylvania&rsquo;s Lehigh Valley, about an hour north of Philadelphia and an hour and a half west of New York City. The campus sits on about 82 acres, and we also maintain a 60-acre arboretum and a 40-acre environmental field station/wildlife sanctuary. Allentown is Pennsylvania's third largest city, and the Lehigh Valley has attracted a number of corporations, dining establishments and entertainment outlets in recent years.&nbsp;
            <a title="Home%20%BB%20Muhlenberg%20College%20%BB%20About%20Us%20KD%20%BB%20About%20the%20Region" class="red" href="/main/aboutus/about-the-region/">
             Learn more about our region
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#what-are-expenses2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="what-are-expenses2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="what-are-expenses">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">
            What are the current expenses for tuition, room, board, and fees?
           </p>
           <p class="panel-title pull-right">
            <span class="fa fa-plus-square-o">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
           </div>
          </div>
         </a>
         <div id="what-are-expenses2" class="panel-collapse collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="what-are-expenses">
          <div class="panel-body">
           <p>
            For the&nbsp;2016-2017 school year, tuition &amp; fees are $48,310; room &amp; board are $10,970. The total cost of $59,280 covers all but books and personal expenses for all students in or out of state.
            <a title="Home%20%BB%20Muhlenberg%20College%20%BB%20Admissions%20KD%20%BB%20Costs%20and%20Opportunities" class="red" href="/main/admissions/costsandopportunities/">
             More about costs and opportunities
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#types-financial-aid2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="types-financial-aid2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="types-financial-aid">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">
            What types of financial aid are available?
           </p>
           <p class="panel-title pull-right">
            <span class="fa fa-plus-square-o">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
           </div>
          </div>
         </a>
         <div id="types-financial-aid2" class="panel-collapse collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="types-financial-aid">
          <div class="panel-body">
           <p>
            The primary component of a student's financial aid package is the Muhlenberg College Grant, which does not need to be paid back and is renewable for four years based on academic progress and family financial circumstances. In addition, the Stafford Loan, Perkins Loan, Pell Grant, and Supplemental Educational Opportunity Grant are all available based on family need. Entering freshmen are eligible for Merit Scholarships in amounts ranging up to $18,000 per year. In addition, the three honors programs each carry $4,000 annual awards, and performing and fine arts talent grants are awarded on the basis of an audition or submitted portfolio. Read the
            <a href="/main/admissions/therealdealonfinancialaid/">
             Real Deal on Financial Aid
            </a>
            . And get more details about our grants and awards&nbsp;on our
            <a href="/main/aboutus/finaid/applyingforaid/merit.html">
             Merit Scholarship page.
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#kinds-of-tests2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="kinds-of-tests2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="kinds-of-tests">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">
            What kinds of tests are required for admission?
           </p>
           <p class="panel-title pull-right">
            <span class="fa fa-plus-square-o">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
           </div>
          </div>
         </a>
         <div id="kinds-of-tests2" class="panel-collapse collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="kinds-of-tests">
          <div class="panel-body">
           <p>
            If you submit test scores, we ask for the SAT-I or ACT. Since 1996, Muhlenberg has offered prospective freshmen the option of not submitting their SAT or ACT scores, and approximately 10-15% of our applicant pool takes advantage of this option each year. If you opt not to send your scores, you&rsquo;ll need to have a personal interview and submit the SAT-optional form, as well as a graded paper from 11th or 12th grade with the teacher&rsquo;s grade and comments included (no poems or mathematical formulas, please). We also honor AP credit. One final note, if you&rsquo;re applying for a cooperative program or wish to be considered for merit scholarships, you must submit test scores.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#interviews-required2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="interviews-required2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="interviews-required">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">
            Are interviews required?
           </p>
           <p class="panel-title pull-right">
            <span class="fa fa-plus-square-o">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
           </div>
          </div>
         </a>
         <div id="interviews-required2" class="panel-collapse collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="interviews-required">
          <div class="panel-body">
           <p>
            Interviews are highly recommended for all students, and required for students applying for one of our cooperative programs and for students who choose not to submit SAT or ACT scores. Please make advance reservations for interviews and tours by calling 484-664-3200.
            <a title="Home%20%BB%20Muhlenberg%20College%20%BB%20Admissions%20KD%20%BB%20Visit%20with%20us" class="red" href="/main/admissions/visitwithus/">
             More about visiting
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#sit-in-on-class2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="sit-in-on-class2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="sit-in-on-class">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">
            Can I sit in on a class?
           </p>
           <p class="panel-title pull-right">
            <span class="fa fa-plus-square-o">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
           </div>
          </div>
         </a>
         <div id="sit-in-on-class2" class="panel-collapse collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="sit-in-on-class">
          <div class="panel-body">
           <p>
            Yes. If you&rsquo;re a high school senior, you have two options: scheduling a day visit, which is hosted by a current student and may include sitting in on one or two classes and eating in the student dining room; or simply sitting in on a class. We ask for at least two weeks' advance notice for both options.
            <a title="Home%20%BB%20Muhlenberg%20College%20%BB%20Admissions%20KD%20%BB%20Types%20of%20Visits" class="red" href="/main/admissions/types-of-visits/">
             More about visit options
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#social-life2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="social-life2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="social-life">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">
            What is social life like?
           </p>
           <p class="panel-title pull-right">
            <span class="fa fa-plus-square-o">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
           </div>
          </div>
         </a>
         <div id="social-life2" class="panel-collapse collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="social-life">
          <div class="panel-body">
           <p>
            Busy! There are over 100 student organizations on campus, from six a capella singing groups to theater, dance and improv; from intramurals to community service; and from environmental action to writing, polling and political activism. There are also five sororities and three fraternities. And if you don&rsquo;t find what you are looking for, you can start it yourself!
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#honor-ap-credit2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="honor-ap-credit2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="honor-ap-credit">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">
            Does the College honor AP credit?
           </p>
           <p class="panel-title pull-right">
            <span class="fa fa-plus-square-o">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
           </div>
          </div>
         </a>
         <div id="honor-ap-credit2" class="panel-collapse collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="honor-ap-credit">
          <div class="panel-body">
           <p>
            Students who take an Advanced Placement class in high school and who score a 4 or 5 on their AP examination may earn a course unit (one course) toward graduation from Muhlenberg. Students earning a score of 3 on the AP exam may eliminate a required course but will probably not earn a course unit. &nbsp;Muhlenberg also offers credit for International Baccalaureate (IB) testing. &nbsp;
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#honors-programs2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="honors-programs2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="honors-programs">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">
            Are there honors programs?
           </p>
           <p class="panel-title pull-right">
            <span class="fa fa-plus-square-o">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
           </div>
          </div>
         </a>
         <div id="honors-programs2" class="panel-collapse collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="honors-programs">
          <div class="panel-body">
           <p>
            Yes, incoming first-year students may be invited to join one of three honors programs:
            <a class="red" href="http://muhlenberg.edu/main/academics/dana-scholars/">
             Dana Scholars
            </a>
            ,
            <a class="red" href="http://muhlenberg.edu/main/academics/rjfellows/">
             RJ Fellows
            </a>
            ,&nbsp;and
            <a class="red" href="http://muhlenberg.edu/main/academics/muhlenberg-scholars/">
             Muhlenberg Scholars
            </a>
            . Muhlenberg also houses a chapter of
            <a class="red" href="http://muhlenberg.edu/main/aboutus/dean-academic/honors/societies/pbk.html">
             Phi Beta Kappa
            </a>
            and several other
            <a class="red" href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/aboutus/dean-academic/honors/societies/societies.html">
             academic honor societies
            </a>
            .
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="panel panel-default">
         <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#collapse-accordion" href="#academic-support2" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="academic-support2">
          <div class="panel-heading" role="tab" id="academic-support">
           <p class="panel-title pull-left">

In addition, Muhlenberg enjoys unusual religious diversity, with approximately one third Catholic students, one third Jewish students, and just over 20% Protestant students. Organizations such as an active Hillel, the Newman Association, and the Lutheran Student Movement support religious life on campus.
