          <h1>
          </h1>
          <h3 style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Welcome to Canvas!
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Canvas is here and we want to make your transition as smooth as possible. &nbsp;The Canvas website features faculty training guides, advice for students, and all the resources you need to explore Canvas. &nbsp;Keep an eye out for updates via bi-weekly web blogs and follow the Instructional Technology and Digital Learning twitter (@ITDL_Muhlenberg) for frequent updates and training opportunities!
          </p>
          <p>
           &nbsp;
           <strong style="font-size: 1.17em;">
            How do I get started?
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           User friendly and flexible, Canvas' design provides faculty and students the opportunity to personalize their Canvas experience. &nbsp;Whether you are creating a course or exploring a course, we have curated training and documentation to help you out. &nbsp;Check out the Faculty and Student pages to help you get started.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            &nbsp;
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            Need Help?
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Canvas offers fantastic support 24/7 for all your questions: from best practices to missing quizzes. &nbsp;Their live chat and hotline connect within seconds and their follow-up email will direct you to thorough documentation.
          </p>
          <p>
           We are here for you too! The help desk will direct you to the best resources and connect you with a member of the digital learning team for consultations and questions. &nbsp;Check out our training sessions or schedule a One-on-one to discuss your ideas or your specific course needs.
          </p>
