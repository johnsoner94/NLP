      <div class="page-header" style='background-image:url("/media/kdmedia/images/page-headers/virtual-tour.jpg");'>
       <h1>
        Virtual Tour
       </h1>
       <p>
        Can't visit campus in person? Take the virtual tour to get a glimpse of the historic beauty on Muhlenberg's campus
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="cta-section hidden-sm hidden-md hidden-lg" data-sidebar-buttons-target="data-sidebar-buttons-target">
      </div>
      <div class="row">
       <div class="col-xs-12">
        <div class="virtual-tour-container" style="width:100%; height:380px; text-align:center;">
         <iframe class="virtual-tour-iframe" src="https://media.virtualrabbit.com/embed/?id=1054" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="AllowFullScreen">
         </iframe>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="row">
       <div class="col-xs-12">
        <h2 style="color:#333333; margin-bottom:20px;">
         Using the 360 Virtual Tour
        </h2>
       </div>
      </div>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Use the
       <strong>
        full-screen option
       </strong>
       to expand your window.
      </p>
      <p>
       Each scene
       <strong>
        auto rotates
       </strong>
       360 degrees. With the photo,
       <strong>
        click and drag
       </strong>
       to manually move 360&deg; through the scene.
      </p>
      <p>
       Use the
       <strong>
        magnify icons
       </strong>
       to zoom in and out.
      </p>
      <p>
       Look for hot spots marked with
       <strong>
        info bubbles
       </strong>
       ; clicking on these will bring up related information about the building and campus.
      </p>
      <p>
       Visit other buildings using the
       <strong>
        scenes tab
       </strong>
       at the bottom left of the screen or use the
       <strong>
        arrow bubbles
       </strong>
       in the photo to jump to the neighboring building.
      </p>
