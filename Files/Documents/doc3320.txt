          <h1>
           Off-Campus Research and Scholarship Presentations
          </h1>
          <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="EditMainContent" -->
          <div class="content-add10">
           <p>
            Some students have the opportunity to present their research at regional and national conferences and competitions. Many of these presentations are supported by
            <a href="/main/aboutus/dean-academic/travel.html">
             travels funds from the Dean of Academic Life
            </a>
            .
            <br />
            <br />
           </p>
           <div class="content-add05">
            <p>
             <strong>
              2016
             </strong>
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Emily Davidson ('18), Madeleine Kapushy ('19), Melanie Goetz ('17), Thomas&nbsp;Yanushefski ('18), and Catherine Zimel ('17) presented their work as Summer Research Scholars at the Lehigh Valley Health Network during an annual poster session at the Cedar Crest campus of LVHN on July 29, 2016. Melanie Goetz ('17) was awarded third prize for her poster presentation.&nbsp;
              <table border="0">
               <tbody>
