          <h1>
          </h1>
          <div class="content-add10">
           <p>
            <strong>
             Minor Requirements
            </strong>
           </p>
           <span class="info style3">
            A minor in Business Administration consists of seven courses
            <span style="color: #e31635;">
            </span>
            as listed below:
            <br />
            ECN 101 Principles of Macroeconomics
            <strong>
             OR
            </strong>
            <span style="color: #e31635;">
            </span>
            <br />
            ECN 102 Principles of Microeconomics
            <br />
            FIN 237 or 238 Corporation Finance
            <br />
            ACT 101 Financial Accounting
            <br />
            BUS 235 or 236 Management
            <br />
            BUS 239 or 240 Marketing
            <br />
            MTH 119 Statistical Analysis
            <span class="style2">
             OR
            </span>
            <br />
            MTH 331 Mathematical Statistics I
            <span class="style2">
             AND
            </span>
            MTH 332 Mathematical Statistics II
           </span>
           <span class="info style3">
            <br />
            One elective chosen in consultation with the faculty advisor
           </span>
          </div>
