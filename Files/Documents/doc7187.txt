     <div class="titlerow" style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      Core Databases
     </div>
     <ul>
      <li>
       SciFinder
       <br />
       <a href="https://scifinder.cas.org/">
        https://scifinder.cas.org/
       </a>
       <br />
       A database and research tool including literature from many scientific disciplines, specifically in chemistry, engineering, and materials science. **Patrons must register
       <a href="https://origin-scifinder.cas.org/registration/index.html?corpKey=EAED9FB1-86F3-50AB-4A5D-9970D02A6395">
        here
       </a>
       and are required to have a muhlenberg e-mail address to use this database.**
      </li>
      <li>
       American Chemical Society Web Editions
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://pubs.acs.org/action/showPublications?display=journals">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://pubs.acs.org/action/showPublications?display=journals
       </a>
       <br />
       Abstracts and some full-text of peer-reviewed research journals in the chemical and related sciences
      </li>
      <li>
       ScienceDirect College Edition Health and Life Sciences, Physical Sciences
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/
       </a>
       <br />
       A database of 1000+ full-text Elsevier journals in the areas of health, life, and physical sciences
      </li>
      <li>
       PubMed
       <br />
       <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/entrez?db=PubMed">
        http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/entrez?db=PubMed
       </a>
       <br />
       A service of the National Library of Medicine, PubMed includes over 19 million citations for biomedical articles back to 1948 and links to many sites providing full text articles and other related resources.
      </li>
      <li>
       Chemical Abstracts
       <br />
       Located on the B level of the library (540.05), Chemical Abstracts are a print index of scientific literature, technical papers, and patent literature in the fields of chemistry or chemical engineering.
      </li>
      <li>
       Academic Search Complete
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=a9h">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&defaultdb;=a9h
       </a>
       <br />
       Provides full text access to scholarly and non-scholarly publications in a wide variety of disciplines and subject areas.
      </li>
      <li>
       Omnifile
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=ofm">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&defaultdb;=ofm
       </a>
       <br />
       A database with some full text articles covering various academic subjects including general sciences, humanities, social sciences, and business.
      </li>
      <li>
       PubChem
       <br />
       <a href="http://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/">
        http://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/
       </a>
       <br />
       Chemical structures database focused on small organic molecules and their biological activities
      </li>
     </ul>
