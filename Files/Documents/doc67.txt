          <h1>

          <div class="content-add05">
           <p>
            <strong>
             Founded:
            </strong>
            1848
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Accreditation
            </strong>
            :&nbsp; Muhlenberg College is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education, 3624 Market Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104, 267-282-5000.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Affiliation:
            </strong>
            <a href="/main/campuslife/religiouslife/">
             Evangelical Lutheran Church in America
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Enrollment:
            </strong>
            2,200 students (42% men, 58% women)
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Campus:
            </strong>
            82 acres
           </p>
           <p>
            <a title="Home%20%BB%20Muhlenberg%20College%20%BB%20Academics" href="/academics/">
             <strong>
              Academics
             </strong>
            </a>
            : Liberal Arts and pre-professional studies
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="/main/aboutus/finaid/">
             <strong>
              Financial Aid
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Approximately 80% of students receive some form of
             <a href="/main/aboutus/finaid/">
              financial aid
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             Both need-based and
             <a href="/main/aboutus/finaid/applyingForAid/merit.html">
              merit aid
             </a>
             available
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Faculty
            </strong>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             171 full-time
            </li>
            <li>
             123 part-time
            </li>
            <li>
             &nbsp;85% hold Ph.D. or other terminal degree
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Student/Faculty Ratio:
            </strong>
            11:1
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/">
              Library Resources
             </a>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Over 310,000 volumes and 360,000 microforms on-campus
            </li>
            <li>
             Over 1.75 million books via interlibrary loan
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <a href="http://muhlenbergsports.com/" target="_blank">
              Intercollegiate Sports
             </a>
             (Centennial Conference, Division III):
             <br />
             <br />
            </strong>
            A three-level, 39,000 square-foot addition to our
            <a href="http://muhlenbergsports.com/sports/2014/12/12/LSC.aspx?path=facilities_" target="_blank">
             Life Sports Center
            </a>
            opened in August 2004.
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Men-
             </strong>
             football, soccer, cross country, basketball, wrestling, baseball, indoor and outdoor track &amp; field, tennis, lacrosse, and golf.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Women-
             </strong>
             tennis, field hockey, cross country,&nbsp;indoor and outdoor track &amp; field, volleyball, basketball, softball, soccer, lacrosse, and golf.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            (If Varsity sports are not for you, check out our
            <a href="http://muhlenbergsports.com/sports/2015/1/7/Intramural%20Sports%20.aspx" target="_blank">
             Intramural
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://muhlenbergsports.com/sports/2015/1/5/club%20sports.aspx" target="_blank">
             Club Sports
            </a>
            .)
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <a href="/campuslife/">
              Campus Life
             </a>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             100+ clubs and
             <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/campuslife/activities/organizations/list.html" target="_blank">
              student organizations
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             3 national fraternities
            </li>
            <li>
             4 national sororities
            </li>
            <li>
             1 multicultural sorority
            </li>
            <li>
             2 community service fraternities (coed)
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <a href="/main/aboutus/disabilities/accessibility/">
              Campus Accessibility
             </a>
            </strong>

           <p>
            <strong>
             Cost for the 2015-2016 Academic Year
            </strong>
           </p>

             Tuition and Fees $45,680
            </li>
            <li>
             Room and Board $10,595
            </li>
            <li>
             TOTAL: $56,275
            </li>
           </ul>

            <strong>
             2015 Freshman Class Profile
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             (As of July 1, 2015)
            </em>
            Applications Received: 5,014
            <br />
            Students Offered Admission: 2,466
            <br />
            Students Enrolled: 585

            <li>
             <strong>
              High School Class Rank
             </strong>
             <br />
             First Tenth: 41%
             <br />
             Second Tenth: 22%
             <br />
             Third Tenth: 13%
            </li>
           </ul>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              SAT Scores
             </strong>
             <br />
             The Middle 50% of students enrolled in the freshman class had SAT scores approximately between 560 and 660 on the Critical Reading and Writing sections of the SAT, and between 560 and 670 on the Mathematics section of the SAT. Mean ACT Composite score was 28.
            </li>
           </ul>

            <li>

             <br />

             <br />
