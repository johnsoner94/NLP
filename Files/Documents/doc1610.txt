             <strong>
              <span style="color: rgb(192, 0, 0);">
               OVERVIEW OF WEEKEND ACTIVITIES
               <br />
              </span>
             </strong>
             We're looking forward to three fun-filled days! &nbsp;Please make plans to join your classmates for any of the Alumni Weekend activities, especially these highlights:
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              <span style="color: rgb(192, 0, 0);">
               FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 18
              </span>
             </strong>
             <br />
             <strong>
              9:00 a.m. - 11:30 a.m.&nbsp; Ride and Stride
              <br />
             </strong>
             Begin Alumni Weekend with the Ride and Stride - a new tradition started by the Class of 1962 in celebration of our 50th Reunion in 2012. &nbsp;We are still in motion! &nbsp;Bring your bike or your walking shoes for a trip through the campus, to the tree-lined streets, down to the Rose Garden and Cedar Beach... remember the tug of war across the stream? &nbsp;Riders will continue the journey along the beautiful Allentown Parkway. &nbsp;Route is mostly flat with challenge hills available for those who want an additional thrill. &nbsp;
             <a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/rideandstride" target="_blank" title="Ride and Stride Info">
              Click here to learn more about the Ride and Stride.
             </a>
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              12:00 a.m - 1:30 p.m. &nbsp;Lunch - through the line
             </strong>
             <br />
             Join your classmates for lunch in the Wood Dining Commons. &nbsp;We will have a dedicated seating area for the Class of 1962 on the mezzanine level, conveniently accessible by elevator.
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              1:30 p.m. - 2:30 p.m. &nbsp;Leaving Your Legacy (or How to Live Forever): &nbsp;A Brief Overview of Philanthropic Planning
             </strong>
             <br />
             A special session about how to get the most for your money when giving to Muhlenberg. &nbsp;Presented by request by Greg Wilson, Planned Giving &amp; Leadership Gift Officer.&nbsp;
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              1:30 p.m - 5:00 p.m &nbsp;Classes Without Quizzes
             </strong>
             <br />
             Attend unique classes designed and taught by current 'Berg faculty. &nbsp;
             <a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/classes" target="_blank" title="Classes Without Quizzes info">
              Click here for the class schedule.
             </a>
             &nbsp; Don't worry - you won't be tested on this!
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              5:00 p.m - 8:00 p.m &nbsp;Class of 1962 Drinks, Lite Bites, and Camaraderie
             </strong>
             <br />
             Social time at the home of Becky (Lentz) and Rich Gorton, located downstream from the Willows... remember the fraternity parties at the Willows? &nbsp;Join your classmates for drinks and light fare before attending the Decades Party or the Evening of Distinction.
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              7:00 p.m - 9:00 p.m. &nbsp;An Evening of Distinction
              <br />
             </strong>
             Join the Muhlenberg community for An Evening of Distinction honoring alumni achievement and celebrating members of the Henry Melchior Muhlenberg Society (HMMS) and the
             <em>
              Circle of 1848
             </em>
             . &nbsp;
             <a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/distinction" target="_blank" title="An Evening of Distinction information and registration">
              Click here for more information.
             </a>
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              8:00 p.m. - close &nbsp;1960's Decade Party at Grille 3501
             </strong>
             <br />
             Join alumni from the 1960's for more drinks and food at Grille 3501, a lovely restaurant and martini bar with large social area, located near Dorney Park. &nbsp;
             <a href="http://grille3501.com/" target="_blank" title="Grille 3501 information">
              Click here to visit Grille 3501's website.
             </a>
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              <span style="color: rgb(192, 0, 0);">
               SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 19
              </span>
             </strong>
             <br />
             <strong>
              9:00 a.m - 11:00 a.m. &nbsp;President's State of the College Address
              <br />
             </strong>
             Enjoy a continental breakfast, then join President John I. Williams, Jr. for his inaugural Alumni Weekend State of the College Address at 10:00 a.m.
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              11:30 a.m. - 2:00 p.m. &nbsp;Tailgate Lunch
              <br />
             </strong>
             <br />
             <strong>
              12:00 p.m. - 4:00 p.m. &nbsp;Afternoon Mix &amp; Match: &nbsp;Sports and the Arts
             </strong>
             <br />
             Football, Volleyball, Organ Recital, Art Exhibition, etc.
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              2:00 p.m. - 4:00 p.m. &nbsp;Alumni Author Reception
              <br />
             </strong>
             Connect with alumni authors at this special reception hosted by the Trexler Library. &nbsp;
             <strong>
              <em>
              </em>
             </strong>
             <strong>
              <span style="color: rgb(192, 0, 0);">
               Featuring Robert Karp '62 and others.
              </span>
             </strong>
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              3:00 p.m. - 4:30 p.m. &nbsp;Department Alumni Receptions
             </strong>
             <br />
             Connect with faculty, alumni, and students to learn what's new in the academic departments.
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              4:00 p.m. - 6:00 p.m. &nbsp;Fraternity Receptions: &nbsp;TKE and ATO Alumni
             </strong>
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              5:00 p.m. - 8:30 p.m. &nbsp;Class of 1962 Dinner with Heritage Alumni
             </strong>
             <br />
             Join your classmates and other alumni for a special dinner, followed by coffee and mingling. &nbsp;We are very pleased to host our special guests:
             <br />
             <span style="color: rgb(192, 0, 0);">
              <strong>
               Mrs. Georgia Baldrige
              </strong>
             </span>
             , wife of 1962 class advisor and history professor Dr. Ed Baldrige

             <span style="color: rgb(192, 0, 0);">
              <strong>
               Mrs. Aurelia Stella
              </strong>
             </span>
             , romance languages professor, and her husband
             <span style="color: rgb(192, 0, 0);">
              <strong>
               Mr. John Stella
              </strong>
             </span>
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              Special FIREWORKS DISPLAY Saturday night following dinner!
              <br />
             </strong>
             Gather with fellow alumni in Scotty Wood Stadium to enjoy the fireworks. &nbsp;Stadium opens at 8:45 PM, fireworks begin at 9 PM.
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              <span style="color: rgb(192, 0, 0);">
               SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 20
              </span>
             </strong>
             <br />
             <strong>
              10:00 a.m. - 11:15 a.m. &nbsp;Chapel Service and Reunion Alumni Memorial Service
             </strong>
             <br />
             Ecumenical service followed by a special service to remember classmates we have lost.
             <br />
             <br />
             <strong>
              11:15 a.m - 1:00 p.m. &nbsp;Farewell Brunch or Hillel Bagel Brunch
              <br />
             </strong>
             <br />
             These are just some highlights of special events that we think our classmates will enjoy. &nbsp;Check the full Alumni Weekend schedule (
             <a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/aw2015" target="_blank" title="Click here to see the complete Alumni Weekend schedule!">
              click here
             </a>
             or see your Alumni Weekend Brochure) for a complete list of weekend activities.&nbsp;
