              <p style="font-size:135%; color:#7a0109; margin-top: 0px;">
               Diverse. Innovative. Boundary-breaking. First-rate.
              </p>
              <p style="font-size:120%;">
               <b>
                That's the Muhlenberg Theatre &amp; Dance Mainstage series.
               </b>
               Dance performances from intimate contemporary solos to grand-scale ballet. Theatre productions from experimental blackbox to Broadway-style musicals &mdash; all delivered with the
               <b>
                professionalism and vision
               </b>
               of a professional repertory company.
              </p>
              <p style="font-size:120%;">
               <b>
                Muhlenberg's Mainstage represents opportunity,
               </b>
               for patrons and for the students of the Theatre &amp; Dance Department: a vibrant and rambunctous performing arts experience for participants and audiences alike.
              </p>
