       Omnifile
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=ofm">

       </a>
       <br />

       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://muse.jhu.edu/browse">

       </a>
       <br />
       A full-text database with journal articles in the arts, humanities, and social sciences.
      </li>
      <li>
       Sage Journals Online
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://online.sagepub.com/cgi/search">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://online.sagepub.com/cgi/search
       </a>
       <br />
       A database of scholarly journals published by Sage, providing wide coverage in all disciplines, but particularly the social sciences.  Full text for articles published 1999 to the present is linked directly in the database; otherwise, search the library catalog by journal titles to check for the library's other full text or physical copies.
      </li>
      <li>
       Springerlink
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.springerlink.com/">

       <br />
