      <div class="page-header" style='background-image:url("/media/kdmedia/images/page-headers/success-after-berg.jpg");'>
       <h1>
        Success After Muhlenberg
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div class="cta-section hidden-sm hidden-md hidden-lg" data-sidebar-buttons-target="data-sidebar-buttons-target">
      </div>
      <div class="custom-content">
       <p>
        Muhlenberg College was founded upon the ideal that a strong liberal education provides a solid foundation for additional learning and success in almost any endeavor&mdash;and that a truly great education, according to our founder Frederick Augustus Muhlenberg, &ldquo;contemplates the education of one&rsquo;s conscience and the cultivation of one&rsquo;s heart&hellip;&rdquo;
       </p>
       <p>
        This ethos remains at the core of our community.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="row">
       <div class="col-xs-12">
        <h2 style="color:#333333; margin-bottom:20px;">
         Our Network: Four Years Of College, A Lifetime Of Opportunities
        </h2>
       </div>
      </div>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Together, our students, faculty, alumni and parents form a vast and growing network of lifelong career and cause related support.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Career Center
        <br />
       </strong>
       Muhlenberg's Career Center assists students in exploring careers, finding jobs and seeking admission to graduate school. Students are encouraged to begin using the office as early as their first year.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        The Career Resource Library
       </strong>
       houses an extensive collection of books, directories and job-related information. Programs like "Where the Jobs Are," "Interview Like A Pro" and "Writing Awesome Cover Letters" help students enhance job-seeking skills. Students interested in continuing their education will appreciate the Career Center's comprehensive graduate/professional school tips and resources.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Internships
       </strong>
       <br />
       Our community finds that lasting opportunities for success begin with an internship as early as possible in our students&rsquo; college careers. We view the internship matching process holistically. Students, faculty, staff, alumni and parents work together to ensure that each student finds multiple ways to connect to the internships they seek to explore.
      </p>
      <p>
       This process often begins at The Career Center, where students search through our comprehensive database of fields, disciplines and cities. At other times, students work one-on-one with their advisor, other faculty members or alumni to set goals and define the optimal paths forward.
      </p>
