            <b>
             Students and faculty in the Department of Theatre &amp; Dance have a wealth of facilities available to them, including:
            </b>
           </p>

            <li>

             <b>
              large proscenium stages
             </b>

             <li>

               <b>

             </li>

               <b>
                The Paul C. Empie Theatre
               </b>

             </li>

             <a href="studio.html">
              <b>
               studio theatres
              </b>
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             Six
             <b>
              dance and acting studios
             </b>
            </li>
            <li>
             A spacious
             <i>
              new
             </i>

               Rehearsal House

             facility

             <a href="production.html">
              <b>
               production facilities
              </b>
             </a>
            </li>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Well-appointed costume shop
             </li>
             <li>
              A fully equipped scene shop
             </li>
             <li>
              Up-to-date scenic design computing equipment
             </li>
             <li>
              State-of-the-art lighting equipment
             </li>
            </ul>
            <li>
             <b>
              A Pilates studio
             </b>
            </li>
            <li>
             A new
             <b>
              video editing studio
             </b>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            The department is housed in the Trexler Pavilion for Theatre and Dance and the adjoining Dorothy and Dexter Baker Center for the Arts.
           </p>
           <p>
