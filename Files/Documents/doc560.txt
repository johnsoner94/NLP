          <h1>
          </h1>
          <p align="center">
           <span class="content-add05">
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Muhlenberg-Language-Department/273748692668226" target="_blank">
             <img alt="Languages Facebook page" border="0" height="29" src="http://muhlenberg.edu/images/main/academics/llc/facebook2_000.jpg" style="float: right;" width="29" />

           &zwnj;&zwnj;&zwnj;

           &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
           <a href="/main/academics/llc/missionstatement/">
            Mission Statement
           </a>
           <br />
           <a href="/main/academics/llc/whylearnalanguage/">
            Why Learn a Language?
           </a>
           <br />
           <a href="/media/contentassets/pdf/academics/llc/foreign.pdf">
            Careers with Languages
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The Department of Languages, Literatures and Cultures at Muhlenberg College offers students the opportunity to acquire and advance communicative skills in:
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/languages/arabic/">
             Arabic
            </a>
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/languages/chinese/">
             Chinese
            </a>
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/languages/french/">
             French
            </a>
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/languages/german/">
             German
            </a>
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/languages/hebrew/">
             Hebrew
            </a>
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/languages/italian/">
             Italian
            </a>
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/languages/russian/">
             Russian
            </a>
           </strong>
           &nbsp;and
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/languages/spanish/">
             Spanish
            </a>
           </strong>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Students can
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/majorsandminors/">
             major or minor
            </a>
           </strong>
           in French, Russian and Spanish,&nbsp;
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/majorsandminors/">
             minor
            </a>
           </strong>
           in German and Italian and receive a
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/majorsandminors/teachercertification/">
             Teacher Certification
            </a>
           </strong>
           in French, German and Spanish.&nbsp;
          </p>
          <p>
           The
           <strong>
            <a href="/main/academics/llc/learningcenter/">
             Language Learning Center
            </a>
           </strong>
           provides information, technological resources and professional expertise that facilitate the effective use of technology for the study of languages, literatures and cultures.
          </p>
          <p>
           All of our courses and degree programs enhance the international dimension of students&rsquo; liberal-arts education. We strongly encourage students to participate in study abroad programs. For more information, visit the
           <strong>
            <a target="_blank" href="/main/academics/global-education/">
             Office of Global Education
            </a>
           </strong>
