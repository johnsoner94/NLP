     <article class="article-bucket content">
      <div class="story">
       <p>
        PHILADELPHIA (CBS) &#8212; The season will wrap up for the Division III Delaware Valley University football team on Saturday as they Aggies visit Muhlenberg as part of the second annual Centennial-MAC Bowl Series.
       </p>
       <p>
        DelVal was once again one of the top teams in the region, going 8-2 and wrapping up the regular season last week with an impressive 45-7 win over rival Widener.
       </p>
       <p>
        &#8220;8-2 is a solid season and I&#8217;m really proud of our seniors,&#8221; DelVal head coach Duke Greco tells KYW Newsradio. &#8220;I know, as a team, we&#8217;re disappointed we&#8217;re not in the NCAA Playoffs but another postseason game to honor our seniors, represent the program is a big plus here.&#8221;
       </p>
       <div class="shortcode shortcode-clip-player">
        <iframe src="http://embed.radio.com/clip/61219374?ref_url=http%3A%2F%2Fphiladelphia.cbslocal.com%2F2016%2F11%2F17%2Fdelval-visits-muhlenberg-saturday-in-centennial-mac-bowl-series%2F%3Futm_source%3DDailyContInfoNewsletters%26utm_medium%3DDailyContInfoNewsletters%26utm_campaign%3DDailySportsHeadlines%26utm_content%3D10232&amp;station_id=533&amp;rollup_ga_id=UA-2438645-53&amp;ads_ga_page_tracker=UA-17434257-35" width="345px" height="194px" scrolling="no">
        </iframe>
       </div>
       <p>
        The Aggies average 35.9 points with sophomore quarterback Dashawn Darden leading the way. The Cardinal O&#8217;Hara product has thrown for 1,793 yards and 16 touchdowns with just seven interceptions. Darden has also rushed for 435 yards and eight more scores.
       </p>
