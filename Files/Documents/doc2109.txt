          <h1>
          </h1>
          <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="EditMainContent" -->
          <div class="content-add05" style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <p>
            <strong>
             Special Offerings &amp; Opportunities
             <br />
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The Art Department supports students in participating in a wide range of opportunities including interdisciplinary courses, internships, independent study, study abroad, and student clubs.
            <strong>
             <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
              <br />
             </span>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Special Course Offerings
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The Art Department offers new courses every year through college-wide interdisciplinary programs. Courses include
            <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/academics/writing/first-yearseminars/" target="_blank">
             First Year Seminars
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="https://capstone2.muhlenberg.edu/clusters/clusterList.php" target="_blank">
             Clusters
            </a>
            (paired courses), and
            <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu/aboutus/toc/muhlenbergintegratedlearningabroad/" target="_blank">
             MILA
            </a>
            s (short-term study abroad). Below are some examples of past offerings. Courses change on a yearly basis. To learn about current courses visit capstone or contact the art department.
           </p>
           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            <strong>
            </strong>
            <strong>
            </strong>
            <strong>
             Community Sustainability &amp; Field Studio in Costa Rica
            </strong>
            (MILA short-term study abroad in Studio Art &amp; Biology)
           </p>
           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            <strong>
            </strong>
            <strong>
             Documentary Photography
            </strong>
            (team-taught course in Studio Art and Media&amp; Communications) Students produced a multi-media documentary website
            <a href="http://slatestories.net/" target="_blank">
             Slate Stories.
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            <strong>
             Paper and Ink
            </strong>
            (Cluster courses in Studio Art &amp; History). This cluster asks students both to examine book production in its historical context and to create contemporary artist's books through the process of printmaking. Students in the cluster will produce art in dialogue with historical practices, and consider the history of the design, production, and reception of printed words and images.
           </p>
           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            <strong>
             Art in the Frame
            </strong>
            (Cluster courses in Studio Art &amp; Theatre). This cluster explores different modes of creative expression inside the visual "frames" of art and theatre, stemming from narrative, observational, experiential, and/or conceptual positions.
           </p>
           <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
            <strong>
             Art, Experience, and the Irrational
            </strong>
            (First Year Seminar in Studio Art)
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Internships &amp; Independent Study
             <br />
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Many of our students pursue areas of individual interest through internships and independent study.
            <a href="http://catalog.muhlenberg.edu/preview_entity.php?catoid=2&amp;ent_oid=103" target="_blank">
             Internships
            </a>
            are available through a range of art organizations and may be completed for credit in consultation with a faculty advisor. Highly motivated students may be eligible for grants to work one-on-one with an Art Department faculty member for a 4-8 week period over the summer. In addition, students have developed independent studies to explore specific topics in depth (recent independent studies include textile printing and Roman art history), to develop scholarly articles, and even to curate exhibitions. Students have also included studio art and art history courses as an integral part of interdisciplinary
            <a href="http://catalog.muhlenberg.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=2&amp;poid=161&amp;returnto=47" target="_blank">
             self-designed majors
            </a>
            .
            <strong>
             <br />
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             LVAIC
             <br />
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Through Muhlenberg&rsquo;s
            <strong>
             Lehigh Valley Association of Independent Colleges
            </strong>
            partnership
            <a href="https://www.lvaic.org/default.aspx?pageid=395" target="_blank">
             (LVAIC)
            </a>
            , students may also enroll in art classes at nearby Cedar Crest College, DeSales University, Lafayette College, Lehigh University, and Moravian College. Students have studies graphic design, and metalworking.
            <strong>
             <br />
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Study Abroad
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The Art Department encourages our students to study abroad. Some of the most popular study abroad destinations for studio art and art history students include the Florence University of the Arts, the Slade School of Fine Arts in London and Goldsmith&rsquo;s College in London. Other art-related programs include SIT&rsquo;s Arts and Social Change series in the Czech Republic or Indonesia, as well as their National Identity and the Arts program in India or Senegal. Please visit our Office of
            <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/academics/global-education/" target="_blank">
             Global Education
            </a>
            for a complete listing of Muhlenberg-approved offerings available to all students.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Student Clubs
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/muhlenbergmuses" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              MUSES
             </strong>
            </a>
            is Muhlenberg's art and literary magazine. In addition to a yearly publication featuring both writing and art, MUSES hosts several open-mic nights per year.
            <span>
             Join the email list at
             <a href="mailto:muhlenbergmuses@gmail.com" target="_blank">
              muhlenbergmuses@gmail.com
             </a>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            The&nbsp;
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Muhlenberg-Jewelry-Club-1526195720987740/home" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              Jewelry Making Club
             </strong>
            </a>
            <span>
             explores the wide range of jewelry making techniques from beading to metal work, and is always open to introduce new methods. One does not need any previous jewelry making knowledge to join. Contact&nbsp;
             <a href="mailto:JewelryMakingClub.GWS1_PO.GWS1@gws1.muhlenberg.edu" target="_blank">
              JewelryMakingClub.GWS1_PO.GWS1@gws1.muhlenberg.edu
             </a>
             .
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            &nbsp;
           </p>
