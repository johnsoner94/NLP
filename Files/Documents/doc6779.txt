           <h1>
            A Message from the Chair of the Muhlenberg Board of Trustees
           </h1>
           <p>
            Dear Members of the Muhlenberg College community:
           </p>
           <p>
            With the recent announcement of President Helm&rsquo;s retirement, I wanted to reach out to the Muhlenberg College community to comment on the announcement and inform you about the presidential search process.
           </p>
           <p>
            We will have many opportunities to celebrate the success of Randy&rsquo;s presidency. Celebration is very appropriate because Muhlenberg is academically and financially strong. The hard work of the past ten years has yielded outstanding results. We enter the search for our next President from a position of strength built through a strong and effective leadership team, a dedicated staff, extraordinary faculty, a proud alumni contingent and an outstanding student body. Randy has noted that there is still a great deal to be done during his remaining time as president. I agree with him and have total confidence that with his ongoing leadership and the support of the Muhlenberg community the College will continue to thrive.
           </p>
           <p>
            We are in the process of bringing together a search committee that will include trustee, alumni, faculty, staff and student representation. Trustee Eric L. Berg &rsquo;78 will chair the search committee.
           </p>
           <p>
            An executive search firm will be selected in the near future. We are extremely confident that the Muhlenberg College presidency will be very attractive to an excellent pool of accomplished leaders. We fully expect to have a new president selected, and on the job, by July 1, 2015.
           </p>
           <p>
            While it is important to respect the confidentiality of the candidates, we will attempt to make the process as transparent and open as possible. The College will have a presidential search website that will contain all pertinent, public information about the search. This will include committee members&rsquo; backgrounds, contact information as well as search firm material. It will also include an opportunity to nominate candidates. Most importantly, we plan to provide periodic updates about the committee&rsquo;s work and progress.
           </p>
           <p>
            As a member of the Muhlenberg College community, you should feel extremely proud. The next President of Muhlenberg will have the opportunity to build on an extremely strong foundation and a long history of success. There is no question that the future is extremely bright. Thanks to all of you who continue to support this wonderful institution.
           </p>
           <p>
            Sincerely,
            <br />
            Richard C. Crist, Jr. '77, P'05, P'09
           </p>
