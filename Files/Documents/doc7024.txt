     <div class="titlerow" style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      Core Resources for Finding Articles and Books
     </div>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Anthropology Plus
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&amp;defaultdb=ant">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.ebscohost.com/login.asp?profile=ehost&defaultdb;=ant
       </a>
       <br />
       Citations to scholarly journals and books in anthropology, archaeology, and related fields.  Use the Check for Full Text buttons to check for full text online or in the library.
      </li>
      <li>
       AnthroSource
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.anthrosource.net/">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.anthrosource.net/
       </a>
       <br />
       A database of mostly scholarly journal articles, as well as some books and non-scholarly articles published by the American Anthropological Association.  Some full text is linked directly in the database; otherwise, search the library catalog by journal titles to check for full text online or in the library.
      </li>
      <li>
       JSTOR
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.jstor.org/">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.jstor.org/
       </a>
       <br />
       A database of scholarly journals for most academic disciplines. The most recent three to five years of most journals are not included.  Full text is linked directly in the database.
      </li>
      <li>
       Encompass Search
       <br />
       <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/">
        http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/
       </a>
       <br />
       Encompass Search finds information by searching through a massive collection of books and scholarly journals, as well as newspapers and magazines, dissertations, conference proceedings, music, video, maps, and more.
      </li>
     </ul>
    </div>
    <div style="clear:both;">
    </div>

   <div id="content_box_10269306" class="content_box content_box_3">
    <div style="display:block;">
     <div class="titlerow" style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      Core Resources for Finding Background Information
     </div>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Credo Reference
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.credoreference.com">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.credoreference.com
       </a>
       <br />
       Hundreds of dictionaries, encyclopedias, fact books, guides, companions and more from respected publishers across dozens of major subject areas, including many notable titles for anthropology. Full text is linked directly in the database.
      </li>
      <li>
       21st Century Anthropology: A Reference Handbook
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://find.galegroup.com/gvrl/infomark.do?type=aboutBook&amp;prodId=GVRL&amp;eisbn=9781412979283&amp;userGroupName=mul_coll&amp;version=1.0">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://find.galegroup.com/gvrl/infomark.do?type=aboutBook&prodId;=GVRL&eisbn;=9781412979283&userGroupName;=mul_coll&version;=1.0
       </a>
       <br />
       Highlights the most important anthropology topics, issues, questions, and debates in the 21st century. Includes applied anthropology, archaeology and paleontology, sociocultural anthropology, evolution, linguistics, physical and biological anthropology, and primate studies.  Full text online.
      </li>
      <li>
       Encyclopedia of Anthropology
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://find.galegroup.com/openurl/openurl?url_ver=Z39.88-2004&amp;url_ctx_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:ctx&amp;req_dat=info:sid/gale:ugnid:mul_coll&amp;res_id=info:sid/gale:GVRL&amp;ctx_enc=info:ofi:enc:UTF-8&amp;rft_val_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:book&amp;rft_id=info:sid/gale:bmcode:recid/0SDW">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://find.galegroup.com/openurl/openurl?url_ver=Z39.88-2004&url;_ctx_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:ctx&req;_dat=info:sid/gale:ugnid:mul_coll&res;_id=info:sid/gale:GVRL&ctx;_enc=info:ofi:enc:UTF-8&rft;_val_fmt=info:ofi/fmt:kev:mtx:book&rft;_id=info:sid/gale:bmcode:recid/0SDW
       </a>
       <br />
       Over 1,000 entries that focus on topics in physical/ biological anthropology, archaeology, cultural/social anthropology, linguistics, and applied anthropology. Also included are relevant articles on geology, paleontology, biology, evolution, sociology, psychology, philosophy, and theology.  Full text online.
      </li>
     </ul>
