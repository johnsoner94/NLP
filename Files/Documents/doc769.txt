             &zwnj;&nbsp;to access our Youtube video archives page and then click on the video you are interested in viewing. It is as easy as that!
            </li>

             <a href="/cultural/ijcu/lentenclergyworkshop/">
              <em>
               <strong>
                <span class="redtext">
                 <span style="color: #ff0000;">
                  Lenten Clergy (Pre-Lent) Workshop
                 </span>
                </span>
               </strong>
              </em>

              &zwnj;
             </strong>

            </li>
            <li class="graytext">
             <a href="/cultural/ijcu/livingroomdialogue/">

                </span>

              Kristallnacht.
             </em>

             </strong>
             <span class="redtext">
              <span class="style12">
               <span style="color: #ff0000;">
               </span>
               - Moyer Hall, Miller Forum (lecture is free &amp; open to the public)
               <strong>
                <span style="color: #ff0000;">
                 @ 4:30
                </span>
               </strong>
               <strong>
                <span>
                 &nbsp;p.m.

               </strong>

               <strong>

                </span>
               </strong>
               Dinner: Honoree, Rabbi Allen Juda

             </span>

              <span class="style12">
               - Seegers Union, Great Room (There is a cost associated with this event.)
              </span>
             </span>
