&lt;p style="font-size:140%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:20px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;&lt;i&gt;Gypsy&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;Music by Jule Styne, Lyrics by Stephen Sondheim, Book by Arthur Laurents&lt;br  /&gt;
Director: Charles Richter&lt;br  /&gt;
Musical Director: Michael Schnack&lt;br  /&gt;
Choreographer: Karen Dearborn
&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;Rehearsals are May 24 &ndash; June 14:&lt;br  /&gt;
Tuesday &ndash; Sunday: 1&ndash;5 p.m. and 7&ndash;11 p.m.&lt;br  /&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;Performances are June 15 &ndash; July 3&lt;br  /&gt;
Wednesday &ndash; Saturday at 8 p.m.&lt;br  /&gt;
Sunday at 2 p.m.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;Performers ages 16 and up are encouraged to audition. There are several roles for older actors. &lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;In addition we will be casting children, both girls and boys, between the ages of 8 and 12, under 5'2&quot; in height. Young actors will not be called during school hours and will generally be released by 9 pm. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;Note that there is a separate audition time for children on Sunday, Feb. 28. &lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:10px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;Preliminary audition requirements for all auditionees (children and adults)&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;ul style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 40px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Please prepare a 32-bar uptempo song from a Broadway musical written before 1975. Please no rock or pop selections.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Some roles do not require singing but everyone interested in being in the production should prepare a vocal audition.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Bring properly marked sheet music.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;An accompanist will be provided&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;All females auditioning for the production will be required to do a short dance audition. All males auditioning for the production under the age of 30 will be required to do a dance audition. No preparation is required. Males over the age of 30 need not do a dance audition.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:140%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:20px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;&lt;i&gt;In the Heights&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;
Music and Lyrics by Lin-Manuel Miranda, Book by Quiara Alegria Hudes&lt;br  /&gt;
Director: James Peck&lt;br  /&gt;
Musical Director: Ed Bara&lt;br  /&gt;
Choreographer: Samuel Antonio Reyes&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;Rehearsals are June 21 &ndash; July 12:&lt;br  /&gt;
Tuesday &ndash; Sunday: 1&ndash;5 p.m. and 7&ndash;11 p.m.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;Performances are July 13-31&lt;br  /&gt;
Wednesday &ndash; Saturday at 8 p.m.&lt;br  /&gt;
Sunday at 2 p.m.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;We will be casting actors ages 15 and up. Actors of color are especially encouraged to audition for the production.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:10px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;Preliminary audition requirements&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;ul style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 40px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Please prepare a 32-bar cut of a song from a contemporary musical.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Bring properly marked sheet music.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;An accompanist will be provided&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;All actors will also be required to do a dance audition.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:120%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:20px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;Auditionees may audition for both productions. People who are auditioning for both productions should prepare two different songs.&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
  
  

&lt;p style="font-size:140%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:40px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;Preliminary Audition Schedule&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:120%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:10px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;Sunday, Feb. 28:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 40px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;1&ndash;3 p.m.: Children's Audition for &lt;i&gt;Gypsy&lt;/i&gt;. The children will sing their prepared song and will be taught a dance combination.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 40px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;3&ndash;11 p.m.: Adult preliminary auditions for both &lt;i&gt;Gypsy&lt;/i&gt; and &lt;i&gt;In the Heights: dance and singing.&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:120%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:10px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;Monday, Feb. 29:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 40px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;5&ndash;8 p.m.: Preliminary dance auditions for both productions.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 40px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;7&ndash;11 p.m.: Preliminary vocal auditions for both productions.&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:120%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:20px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;All auditions will take place at Muhlenberg College at the Baker Center for the Arts and the Trexler Pavilion for Theatre &amp; Dance.&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:120%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:20px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;All actors participating in Muhlenberg Summer Music Theatre productions are paid a stipend. Out-of-town actors are provided with free housing. We will consider casting Equity members on guest artist contracts.&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:140%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:40px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;To Schedule an Audition Appointment&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;Please email your name, your cell phone number, your age, and any restrictions to your availability during the audition period to &lt;a href="mailto:smtcompany@muhlenberg.edu"&gt;smtcompany@muhlenberg.edu&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-left: 20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;You will receive a confirmation email with your appointment time and audition location.&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:140%; margin-left: 0px; margin-top:40px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;Video Auditions&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;If you live too far away from the Allentown area, or you are unable to attend the auditions, you may submit a preliminary video audition. The DVD should consist of a comedic monologue not more than two minutes in length, one vocal selection (see guidelines above), and a 90-second dance solo.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;Please send a link to a video hosted on the internet; e.g., YouTube or Vimeo. &lt;b&gt;Do not send attached video files via email.&lt;/b&gt; You may also submit a DVD following the same guidelines, which should be received prior to the audition dates.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;span style="font-weight: bold"&gt;If you receive a callback you MUST attend the callbacks in person to be considered for the role. Callbacks will be announced soon.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; margin-left:15px;"&gt;We are so excited to see your auditions for &lt;i&gt;Growl!&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;About Doppelskope &amp; &lt;i&gt;Growl&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; margin-left:15px;"&gt;Doppelskope creates highly visual and interactive performances, using puppetry, clown, improvisation, magical illusions, music, circus and philosophy. Our shows explore topics from existential dread to ecological crises. We believe that the stories we tell about the world can change the world. We believe in the intelligence of children and the whimsy of adults. We aim to create work that celebrates life and inspires connection, discovery and community. Learn more about us and our past shows at &lt;a href="http://www.doppelskope.com" target="_blank"&gt;www.doppelskope.com&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; it may be helpful in preparing for your audition to familiarize yourself with our work.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; margin-left:15px;"&gt;We are currently developing &lt;i&gt;Growl!&lt;/i&gt;, a new family musical. The show will include puppetry, physical theatre, music, audience interaction, and will focus on how every person makes an impact on other people and the world around us.  We need high energy performers who work well in an ensemble and are able to learn puppetry and clown skills. For your audition, we'd like to see 1) a short performance piece and 2) hear you sing.  Details on both are below.&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; margin-left:15px;"&gt;&lt;b&gt;1. Prepare a short piece&lt;/b&gt; (no more than a minute and a half) in which you tell us the story of the Goldilocks. Please incorporate physical/visual components, such as your body and/or other objects, and any other tools/skills that are fun for you!  Keep in mind that our shows don't have fourth walls, so there's no need to pretend we're not there in the room with you. Feel free to address us directly and make eye contact with us if you're speaking to "the audience".  Another hint: don't rely too much on text; feel free to use it if you really want to, but think about the many other ways you can tell a story. You don't even have to tell Goldilocks in a linear or literal way if a fun idea takes you in another direction.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; margin-left:15px;"&gt;We'd like to get to know you and we're interested in learning about you as a theatre-maker and creative artist, so keep that in mind as you make your piece! We also want to see you move and we want to see you enjoy yourself.&lt;/p&gt;


&lt;p style="font-size:110%; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; margin-left:15px;"&gt;
&lt;b&gt;2. Prepare a brief part of a song to sing a cappella (about 16 bars).&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
