      <div class="page-header" style='background-image:url("/media/kdmedia/images/page-headers/common-questions.jpg");'>
       <h1>
        Academics
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div class="cta-section hidden-sm hidden-md hidden-lg" data-sidebar-buttons-target="data-sidebar-buttons-target">
      </div>
      <div class="custom-content">
       <p>
        <img src="/media/contentassets/images/academics/Jeff-Pooley-230x230.jpg" alt="Jeff Pooley " style="   margin : 3px;  float : right;" />
        At Muhlenberg, we believe that education is more than a foundation for learning. Our faculty lead students to reach above and beyond, to achieve their goals and to effect change in the professions, passions and interests they pursue.
       </p>
       <p>
        Curiosity and creativity are key to the fabric of Muhlenberg&rsquo;s character. Our professors challenge students to find themselves through experimentation and exploration. Our students join faculty as active collaborators, resulting in research, performance, service and study practically unknown at other undergraduate institutions.
       </p>
       <p>
        &zwnj;
        <img src="/media/contentassets/images/academics/JuliaBurns-AmyHark-230x230.jpg" alt="Julia Burns - Amy Hark" style="   margin : 6px;  float : left;" />
        Muhlenberg&rsquo;s classical liberal arts experience is enriched through a rewarding and challenging curriculum. Our academic disciplines span the humanities, fine arts, social sciences and natural sciences and prepare students to excel in fields as varied as pre-medicine and allied health, pre-law, business, education and pre-theological studies.
       </p>
       <p>
        Here at Muhlenberg, you may find yourself overcoming boundaries and expectations thought impassible. And that sets our graduates apart as lifelong learners and exceptional individuals in the many careers and causes they embrace.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a id="mmc">

       </p>
