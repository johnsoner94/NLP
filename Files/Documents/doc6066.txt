              Readmission for Military Deployment:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Students on leave&nbsp;for military deployment will be readmitted with the same academic status achieved when last attended.&nbsp;Length of absence not to exceed 5 years.&nbsp;
             <em>
              (Exceptions being Veterans with dishonorable discharge, bad conduct discharge or court martial offenses).
             </em>
            </p>
