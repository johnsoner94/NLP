           Things to note:
          </p>

           </li>

            Physical items cannot be shipped
           </li>

            For Interlibrary Loan, add a note that you are studying abroad (for articles, book chapters that we physically own)
           </li>
           <li>
            Can still contact subject librarians for assistance
           </li>
          </ul>
