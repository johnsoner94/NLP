   <h2>
    Find Music Recordings &amp; Scores
   </h2>
   <div id="content_box_14009713" class="content_box content_box_1">
    <div style="display:block;">
     <div class="titlerow" style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      Find Music Recordings and Music Scores
     </div>
     <div style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      <p>
       Search
       <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu" target="_blank">
        Encompass Search
       </a>
       to find music scores and recorded music.
      </p>
      <p>
       To find only music scores and recorded music, use the Music, Sound Recording, or Musical Score limits on the left sidebar of your search results page. Many items will be in Trexler Library; note the call number and status of the item to check its location and availability. For items not in Trexler Library, use the Interlibrary Loan button to request a copy from another library.
      </p>
