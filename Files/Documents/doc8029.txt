           <h3>
            Business Administration with a concentration in Healthcare Management
           </h3>
           <div id="link">
            <p>
             The concentration in Healthcare Management complements a solid grounding in management and leadership, provided in our established accelerated Business Administration curriculum, with six modules (9-14 below) that deliver in-depth application to the specific challenges of the health services industry. Students explore various legal, ethical, financial, marketing and regulatory considerations faced by healthcare managers and develop the sound teamwork and decision-making skills required by&nbsp;successful leaders.&nbsp;Using a project-based learning model,&nbsp;coursework aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the components of today's complex healthcare delivery system.
            </p>
            <p>
             The structure of classes reinforces the importance of experiential and team-based learning. Students are teamed and work together on projects and presentations both inside and outside of the classroom. Students are encouraged and expected to voice their opinions and reflect on their learning. Classes take many forms but generally consist of some lecture with real life application of theory, class discussions, review of assignments and presentations.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Learning Outcomes
              <br />
              <a href="/media/contentassets/pdf/wescoe/programcourseofferings/accelerateddegrees/learningoutcomes/Healthcare Management.pdf">
               Curriculum Road Map - Healthcare Management
              </a>
              <br />
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Upon graduating from Muhlenberg College, students in the
             <strong>
              Accelerated Business Administration Program
             </strong>
             will demonstrate proficiency in the following skills:
             <br />
             <br />
             *Leadership.
             <br />
             *Teamwork.
             <br />
             *Oral and written communication.&nbsp;
             <br />
             *Critical thinking.
             <br />
             *Quantitative analysis.
             <br />
             *Research.
            </p>
            <p>
             Graduates will apply those skills to:
            </p>
            <p>
             *Clearly identify the challenge and implicit aspect of an issue and articulate the integral elements essential to its resolution.
             <br />
             <br />
             *Apply solid business principles in order to propose effective and innovative solutions.
            </p>
            <p>
             *Appreciate the complexity of issues pertaining to globalism and diversity and gain an understanding of their own values and biases.
            </p>
            <p>
             *Understand the ethical dimensions involved in decision making.
            </p>
            <p>
             *Look holistically at organizational issues and make appropriate recommendations.
            </p>
            <p>
             Course Descriptions for the Bachelor's of Business Administration in&nbsp;
             <a href="/media/contentassets/pdf/wescoe/programcourseofferings/accelerateddegrees/stepsheets/WEB - BA-BAHCM 10 REV 021916.pdf">
              Healthcare Management
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/main/wescoe/prospectivestudents/appointment.html">

             If you have any additional questions or would like to speak with an academic advisor, please contact us at 484-664-3300 or

              wescoe@muhlenberg.edu
             </a>
