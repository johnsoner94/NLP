           <h3>
            Airport Shuttle Service
           </h3>
           <div class="content-add05">
            <p>
             Last year we introduced a new airport shuttle service for our students who have a need to travel using the Philadelphia and Newark Airports. After re-evaluating this service, changes have been made.&nbsp; The times listed below are for either airport.&nbsp;
            </p>
            <p>
             The fee for the airport shuttle is $75.00 each way.&nbsp; Reservations must be made at least one week prior to travel.&nbsp; Your reservation will be confirmed via email and payment must be made within one week of confirmation.&nbsp; A copy of your confirmation email must accompany payment made by personal check or money order made payable to Muhlenberg College.&nbsp; Payments must be mailed to Muhlenberg College, Campus Safety, Attn:&nbsp;&nbsp;Steve Goosley, 2400 Chew Street, Allentown, PA 18104.
            </p>
            <p>
             Students must arrive 15&nbsp;minutes prior to departure time.&nbsp; The shuttle will leave promptly at the designated time.&nbsp; If the shuttle is missed, there will be a significantly higher cost since it will be a personal transport.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/main/aboutus/deanst/shuttleservice/airportshuttle/reservationrequestform/">
