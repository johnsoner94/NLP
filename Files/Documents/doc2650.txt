         <div class="roundbox innerbox innerbox_1" id="content21677128">
          <p>
           In partnership with the Digital Learning Team, Trexler Library is pleased to announce the availability of the new&nbsp;One Button Studio, a fully-featured digital video production studio that is designed to be both powerful and easy to use, requiring no prior video production experience. The One Button Studio, located on the B-level of Trexler Library diagonally across from the elevator, is available for use by all Muhlenberg faculty, staff, and students.
          </p>
          <p>
           Developed and designed by Penn State, the One Button Studio allows students to create videos with the ease of just one button.
          </p>
          <p>
           The One Button Studio is a simplified video recording setup that can be used without any previous video production experience. The design of the studio allows you to create high-quality and polished video projects without having to know anything about lights and cameras. You only need to bring your flash drive with you and push a single button.
          </p>

           To learn more about the One Button Studio concept:&nbsp;&nbsp;

          <h2>
           Quick Start Guide on Using the One Button Studio
          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="roundbox innerbox innerbox_1" id="content21677159">
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Steps:
           <img src="http://lgimages.s3.amazonaws.com/data/imagemanager/63912/obshowto.jpg" alt="One Button Studio Quick Guide" />
          </p>
          <ol>
           <li>
            You will need a USB flash drive to use the One Button Studio.
           </li>
           <li>
            Set up your presentation, connect your laptop to the projector if you want to project while presenting.
           </li>
           <li>
            Set up the podium or area that you will be presenting. A podium is available to have your notes and remotes nearby.
           </li>
           <li>
            Insert your flash drive into the port. This will initialize the one button system.
           </li>
           <li>
            The room lights will dim and the studio lights will turn on.
           </li>
           <li>
            The computer screen will tell you when the system is ready to begin.
           </li>
           <li>
            Push the large silver button to begin your recording session.
           </li>
           <li>
            Record your presentation, interview, portfolio, or whatever you are working on.
           </li>
           <li>
            When you are done, push the large silver button again to end your recording session.
           </li>
           <li>
            The system will then download the video file to your flashdrive. Please WAIT before removing your flashdrive from the system.
           </li>
           <li>
            The studio lights will turn off and the regular room lights will turn back on.
           </li>
           <li>
            You may now view your video on your own or edit it as needed.
           </li>
          </ol>
