    <div style="display:block;">
     <div class="titlerow" style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      Core Resources for Finding Articles and Books
     </div>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Sociological Abstracts
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.proquest.com/socabs/advanced?accountid=40980">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.proquest.com/socabs/advanced?accountid=40980
       </a>
       <br />
       Citations to scholarly journal articles, books, book chapters, dissertations and more in sociology and related disciplines in the social and behavioral sciences. Use the Check for Full Text buttons to check for full text online or in the library.
      </li>
      <li>
       JSTOR
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.jstor.org/">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.jstor.org/
       </a>
       <br />
       A database of scholarly journals for most academic disciplines, with many sociology journals. The most recent three to five years of most journals are not included. Full text is linked directly in the database.
      </li>
      <li>
       Encompass Search
       <br />
       <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/">
        http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/
       </a>
       <br />
       Encompass Search finds information by searching through a massive collection of books and scholarly journals, as well as newspapers and magazines, dissertations, conference proceedings, music, video, maps, and more.
      </li>
     </ul>
    </div>
    <div style="clear:both;">
    </div>
   </div>
   <div id="content_box_10545654" class="content_box content_box_3">
    <div style="display:block;">
     <div class="titlerow" style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      Core Resources for Finding Background Information
     </div>
     <div style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      <p>
       To find more print and online reference books for general sociology, as well as specific subjects within or related to sociology, search Encompass Search or ask for help.
      </p>
     </div>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Credo Reference
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.credoreference.com">
        https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://search.credoreference.com
       </a>
       <br />
       Hundreds of dictionaries, encyclopedias, fact books, guides, companions and more from respected publishers across dozens of major subject areas, including many notable titles for sociology. Full text is linked directly in the database.
      </li>
      <li>
       <b>
        21st Century Sociology: A Reference Handbook
       </b>
       <br />
       Location: Main Collection (Ref.) 301 T971c vol. 1-2
       <br />
       Highlights the most important sociology topics, issues, questions, and debates in the 21st century.
      </li>
      <li>
       Annual Review of Sociology
       <br />
       <a href="https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/oclc/2244366?databaseList=1708,638">
        https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/oclc/2244366?databaseList=1708,638
       </a>
       <br />
       Literature reviews providing summary and analysis of research in the field of sociology, published annually. Good for an overview of the state of research on a given topic. Full text is available online for 1975-present.
      </li>
      <li>
       <b>
        Encyclopedia of Social Theory
       </b>
       <br />
       Location: Main Collection (Ref.) 301.01 E56o vol. 1-2 (Level B)
      </li>
     </ul>
