       <h2>
        Make a Research Appointment
       </h2>
       <p>
        Whether you are starting your first research project or have written a dozen papers already, you can benefit
            from a one-on-one session with a Muhlenberg College librarian.
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Search
          </strong>
          effectively the library databases and other resources
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Discover
          </strong>
          new research tools
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Coordinate
          </strong>
          your topic with available resources
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Narrow
          </strong>
          a topic via examination of resources
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Evaluate
          </strong>
          resources for appropriateness
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Cite
          </strong>
          sources in a bibliography
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Present
          </strong>
          your work using relevant technology
         </li>
        </ul>
       </p>
       <p>
        The subject specialist librarians welcome your request in person, by telephone, by email, or by IM.
       </p>
