          <h1>
          </h1>
          <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="EditMainContent" -->
          <div class="content-add10">
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <strong>
             Kitchens
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Public Kitchens are located in the following residence halls:
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            &nbsp;
           </p>
           <div class="content-add05">
            <ul>
             <li>
              Prosser
             </li>
             <li>
              Walz
             </li>
             <li>
              Brown
             </li>
             <li>
              Taylor Hall
             </li>
             <li>
              Martin Luther
             </li>
             <li>
              East
             </li>
             <li>
              The Courts
             </li>
             <li>
              Robertson
             </li>
             <li>
              South
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p class="italic">
             <strong>
              All kitchens are equipped with a stove and oven. Most have refrigerators and all but Brown have Microwave Ovens. Students are welcome to use the kitchens at any time. These kitchens are often the sites of floor dinners and celebrations and they are a great place to bake birthday cakes for your friends. Students supply their own cookware and utensils and must clean up after themselves so that everyone can enjoy these facilities.
             </strong>
            </p>
           </div>
