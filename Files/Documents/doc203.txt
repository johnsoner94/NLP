              <p class="" style="margin: 0px; padding: 22px 0px 0px; font-size: 16px; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; border: 0px;">
               The Young Alumni Council (YAC) acts as a liaison for young alumni to the greater Muhlenberg community. They connect alumni within their first ten years of graduation to one another and to opportunities to give back to the College. YAC aims to be a resource for Muhlenberg alumni in their first decade after graduation. Support provided includes: career networking, service opportunities, regional one on one alumni connections &nbsp;and fun reasons to come home to 'Berg.
