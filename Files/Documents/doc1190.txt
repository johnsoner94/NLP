          <div style="font-size:110%;">
           <p style="font-size:120%; line-height:180%;">
            <b>
             <span style="color:#7a0109;">
              Muhlenberg Theatre &amp; Dance students enjoy the best of both worlds:
             </span>
            </b>
            the
            <b>
             artistic discipline and training
            </b>
            of an intense studio program, and the
            <b>
             intellectual rigor and scholarly breadth
            </b>
            of a first-class liberal arts college.
           </p>
           <p style="font-size:120%; line-height:180%;">
            <b>
             All Muhlenberg students
            </b>
            can take Theatre &amp; Dance courses and participate in productions. For students who are serious about pursuing their interest in the performing arts, the Department offers a Theatre Major, a Dance Major and Minor, and a wide variety of dual majors and cross-curricular study programs.
           </p>
           <hr noshade="noshade" size="1" />
           <p>
            <b>
             The academic programs
            </b>
            in both
            <a href="theatre.html">
             <b>
              Theatre
             </b>
            </a>
            and
            <a href="dance.html">
             <b>
              Dance
             </b>
            </a>
            provide students with intensive studio experiences in performance and design, along with a solid background in the history, theory, and literature of stage performance.
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             The program offers extensive opportunities
            </b>
            for students to act, choreograph, dance, design, stage-manage, and direct. In addition to a
            <b>
             regionally acclaimed and ambitious
             <a href="../onstage/mainstage.html">
              Mainstage production season
             </a>
            </b>
            , the Department supports a wide variety of student-initiated studio projects each year, offering extended opportunities for students to explore
            <i>
             all
            </i>
            of their interests and talents.
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             Resident and visiting
             <a href="../aboutus/facstaff.html">
              faculty
             </a>
             in theatre and dance
            </b>
            offer a wealth of diverse professional perspectives and styles for students to experience, as a way of broadening their own journey as artists.
            <b>
             Master classes, lectures, and
             <a href="../onstage/guest.html">
              special performances
             </a>
            </b>
            by
            <b>
             <a href="../aboutus/guestartists.html">
              visiting artists and artists-in-residence
             </a>
            </b>
            offer further opportunities for students to expand their creative horizons.
           </p>
