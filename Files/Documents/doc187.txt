            <h3>
            </h3>
            <img style="float: left; margin-right: 4px; margin-bottom: 4px;" src="/s/1570/images/gid2/editor/societies/loyalty_society_300_x200.png" />
            <span style="font-size: 16px;">
             <a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/s/1570/index.aspx?sid=1570&amp;gid=2&amp;pgid=1600">
              <strong>
               The Loyalty Society
              </strong>
             </a>
            </span>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              The Loyalty Society recognizes the commitment of donors whose annual gifts serve as the cornerstone of Muhlenberg&rsquo;s continued growth as a liberal arts institution for the 21st Century. Gifts to Muhlenberg in any amount for five or more consecutive fund years count toward The Loyalty Society membership and are greatly appreciated. Multiple intervals of loyalty are recognized &ndash; from members who are just starting a tradition of giving annually to members who have shown commitment to Muhlenberg through annual giving of 30 years or more. Membership is sustained as long as gifts are made to the College every fund year (July 1 to June 30) without exception.
              <span style="font-size: 16px;">
               <strong>
                <br />
                <br />
                Contact:
               </strong>
               The Muhlenberg Fund at 800-859-2243 or email
               <a title="HMMS Information" href="mailto:themuhlenbergfund@muhlenberg.edu?subject=HMMS%20Information">
                themuhlenbergfund@muhlenberg.edu
               </a>
               .
               <br />
               <br />
               <hr />
              </span>
             </span>
             <br />
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              <a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/s/1570/index.aspx?sid=1570&amp;gid=2&amp;pgid=685&amp;sparam=society&amp;scontid=0">
               <strong>
                The Henry Melchior Muhlenberg Society (HMMS)
                <img style="float: left; margin-right: 4px; margin-bottom: 4px;" src="/s/1570/images/gid2/editor/societies/hmms_300x200.png" />
               </strong>
              </a>
             </span>
            </p>
            <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            </span>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              The Henry Melchior Muhlenberg Society (HMMS) was founded in 1967 to recognize a very special group of Muhlenberg leaders. Each year, alumni, parents and friends who share a commitment to Muhlenberg&rsquo;s growing distinction join HMMS by making a leadership gift of $1,500 or more.
             </span>
            </p>
            <span style="font-size: 16px;">
             <strong>
              Member Benefits
             </strong>
             <br />
            </span>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              Members of the Henry Melchior Muhlenberg Society are invited to attend the Evening of Distinction dinner at Alumni Weekend each year.&nbsp; You'll also receive College updates from the president and a listing in various publications.
              <br />
              <br />
              <span style="font-size: 16px;">
               <strong>
                Contact:
               </strong>
               Heather Ennis, Associate Director of The Henry Melchior Muhlenberg Society, at 484-664-3748 or
               <a href="mailto:hennis@muhlenberg.edu?subject=HMMS Giving Society">
                hennis@muhlenberg.edu
               </a>
               .
               <br />
               <br />
              </span>
              <hr />
             </span>
            </p>
            <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            </span>
            <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/devel/estate/index.html" target="_blank">
            </a>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              <em>
               <a title="Planned Giving Overview" href="http://muhlenbergcircle1848.org/" target="_blank">
                <strong>
                 Circle of 1848
                 <img style="float: left; margin-right: 4px; margin-bottom: 4px;" src="/s/1570/images/gid2/editor/societies/circle_300_x_200.png" />
                </strong>
               </a>
              </em>
             </span>
            </p>
            <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu/main/devel/estate/index.html" target="_blank">
            </a>
            <span>
             <strong>
             </strong>
            </span>
            <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            </span>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              The
              <em>
               Circle of 1848
              </em>
              recognizes persons who have included philanthropic support of Muhlenberg College as part of their estate planning. To date, more than 400 alumni, parents, friends, faculty and staff have made a bequest provision for the College through their will; made Muhlenberg an ultimate beneficiary of their retirement plan; created a life income gift agreement; or have made some other planned gift arrangement that benefits the College.
             </span>
            </p>
            <span style="font-size: 16px;">
             <strong>
              Member Benefits
             </strong>
             <br />
            </span>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              In addition to the satisfaction of helping Muhlenberg students, members receive a lapel pin, the Society's publications, listings in various College publications and invitations to annual events of recognition including the annual Advent Carol Service and a Summer Theatre event.
              <br />
              <br />
              <span style="font-size: 16px;">
               <strong>
                Contact:
               </strong>
               Gregory Wilson, Director of Leadership and Planned Giving, at 484-664-3224 or
               <a href="mailto:gwilson@muhlenberg.edu?subject=Circle%20of%201848%20Information">
                gwilson@muhlenberg.edu
               </a>
               .
              </span>
              <br />
              <br />
              <hr />
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              <a href="http://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/s/1570/index.aspx?sid=1570&amp;gid=2&amp;pgid=687">
               <strong>
                Lifetime Giving Society
                <img style="float: left; margin-right: 4px; margin-bottom: 4px;" src="/s/1570/images/gid2/editor/societies/lifetime_300x200.png" />
               </strong>
              </a>
             </span>
            </p>
            <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            </span>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 16px;">
              Created in 2007, Muhlenberg&rsquo;s Lifetime Giving Society boasts more than 250 members, including alumni, parents, friends, foundations, corporations, organizations and churches, and was created &ldquo;in recognition of these friends and benefactors who are committed to the same philosophies and ideals as the founders and who, through their foresight and generosity, seek to ensure that the highest quality of education will be available for future generations,&rdquo; as is listed on the head piece of each plaque display in the Haas College Center Rotunda.
             </span>
            </p>
