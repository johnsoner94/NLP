               <b>
                Inaugural Alumni Juried Exhibition of Small Work
               </b>
              </a>
              <br />
              The Martin Art Gallery and Alumni Relations Office are proud to announce the Inaugural Alumni Juried Exhibition of Small Work, September 5th â€“ 30th in the Galleria Lobby of the Baker Center for the Arts.
              <p>
               The artists: Paul Preuss &rsquo;63, Joanne (Strehle) Bast &rsquo;68, Martha (Schlenker) Knouss &rsquo;68, Susan (Sellers) Stanewick &rsquo;68, Ethan Geehr &rsquo;83, Anneliese Juergensen &rsquo;08, and Emma Sanders &rsquo;08 represent a wide spectrum of reunion year classes.&nbsp; Their compelling works of art cover a wide range of subjects and equally diverse media including oil painting, digital art, photography, watercolor and textiles.
              </p>
              <p>
               The exhibition is free and open to the public as well as the campus community, daily 9:00 AM &ndash; 10:00 PM.&nbsp; A reception to honor the artists will be held during Reunion/Homecoming Weekend, Saturday, September 21, 4 &ndash; 6:00 PM.
