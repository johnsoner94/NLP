             How do I get tickets?
            </b>
           </p>

            <b>
             Order Online:
            </b>
           </p>
           <ul style="margin-bottom:10px;">
            <li>
             Order any time!
            </li>
            <li>
             Visa, MasterCard, American Express
            </li>
            <li>
             Single tickets only. For subscriptions, group sales, special requests, and discount orders, call the box office.
            </li>
            <li>
             Small processing fee per ticket.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p style="margin-left: 48px; margin-top:0px;">
            <span class="more">

              <b>

              </b>

           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 130%; margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 12px">
            <b>
             Order by Phone: 484.664.3333
            </b>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Monday &ndash; Friday, noon &ndash; 6 p.m.
            </li>
            <li>
             Two hours before every show
            </li>
            <li>
             Closed during academic holidays
            </li>
            <li>
             Order with your Visa, MasterCard, or American Express
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p style="font-size: 130%; margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 12px">
            <b>
             Order in Person:
            </b>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Monday &ndash; Friday, noon &ndash; 6 p.m.
            </li>
            <li>
             Closed during academic holidays
            </li>
            <li>
             Pay with check, cash, Visa, MasterCard, or American Express
            </li>
            <li>
             Box office is in the bottom level of the Trexler Pavilion for Theatre &amp; Dance, on the south side of Chew Street between 23rd and 26th streets
            </li>
            <li>
             Summer hours begin June 1, Monday through Saturday, 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p style="font-size: 130%; margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 12px">

             Order by Mail:
            </b>
           </p>
           <ul>

             <a href="../connect/mailinglist.html">
              Sign up
             </a>
             to receive your brochure and order form in the mail.
            </li>

             Pay with check, Visa, MasterCard, or American Express.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p style="font-size: 150%; margin-top: 30px; margin-bottom: 0px; color: #7a0109;">
            <b>
             How much do they cost?
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 130%; margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 12px; ">
            <b>
             Theatre &amp; Dance Mainstage
            </b>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Adults, musicals: $22
            </li>
            <li>
             Adults, all other shows: $15
            </li>
            <li>
             Subscribers (4+ shows): $12
            </li>
            <li>
             Youth, 17 and under (all shows): $8
            </li>
            <li>
             Campus Price (see below): $8
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p style="font-size: 130%; margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 12px; ">
            <b>
             Summer Music Theatre
            </b>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             First 4 Performances of each Mainstage Show:
             <ul>
              <li>
               Adults: $33
              </li>
              <li>
               Seniors: $29
              </li>
              <li>
               Youth/Student: $18
              </li>
             </ul>
            </li>
            <li>
             All Remaining Performances (beginning on the 1st Sunday):
             <ul>
              <li>
               Adults: $39
              </li>
              <li>
               Seniors: $36
              </li>
              <li>
               Youth/Students: $20
              </li>
             </ul>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Children's Show:
             <ul>
              <li>
               All tickets are general admission
              </li>
              <li>
               $10 in June, $12 in July
              </li>
             </ul>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p style="font-size: 130%; margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 12px;">
            <b>
             Group Sales
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 24px;">
            Groups of 15 or more get a great price on tickets &mdash; just $26 for Summer Music Theatre mainstage productions, just $6 or $8 for the Summer Theatre Children's Show, just $16 for the Department Mainstage musical, and just $13 for other  Mainstage productions. To make arrangements, call General Manager Jess Bien at 484.664.3087.
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 130%; margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 12px;">
            <b>
             Campus Price
            </b>
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left: 24px;">
            If you're a member of the Muhlenberg campus community &mdash; students, faculty, and staff &mdash; you can purchase Department Mainstage tickets for
            <span class="style2">
             just $8
            </span>
            <span style="font-size: 120%">
             .
            </span>
           </p>

             </li>
             <li>
              DeSales University

             <li>
              Lafayette College

             <li>
              Lehigh University
             </li>
             <li>
              Moravian College
             </li>
            </ul>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="margin-left: 24px;">
             The campus price is  not available for Summer Music Theatre Shows.
            </span>
